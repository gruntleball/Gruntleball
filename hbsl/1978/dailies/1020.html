Rest assured, it will be dramatic... The Ringlings rally in the seventh inning, trying to avert a loss for Dave Rozema, but Rozema falters in the bottom of the inning and looks doomed to take his only loss of the year. After a moving dugout speech from Art Howe, the team rallies off of John D'Acquisto with three straight hits to tie the game, followed by Cesar Cedeno flying out deep to left to put Ringling ahead. D'Acquisto, frustrated at having lost the lead, but moreso from ensuring that Dave Rozema doesn't lose, he walks Reggie Smith and Graig Nettles before giving up a grand slam to Jason Thompson.
