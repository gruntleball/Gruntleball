weekly awards for the week ending july 26


GOOD TEAM OF THE WEEK:
The CENTURY ATOMS (Randy Stearns), Okoboji Cropdusters (Bryan Smith) and
Felldownhead Firsts (Doug English) went 5-1.  Century slightly bested
Felldownhead in terms of run margin, +13 to +11.  The 4-3 Winnetka Bahai
(Carroll/Kleine) led the league with 52 runs scored.  The 3-3 Barcelona
Brewers  (Al Marshall) led the league with 14 runs allowed.  The 4-3
Cincinnati Reds (Chuck Miller) led the league in margin with +14.

BAD TEAM OF THE WEEK:
The ROCKY MOUNTAIN OYSTERS (Haverty/Mojo) went 0-6 and have lost 10 
straight, scoring 18 runs and giving up 31.  The 2-4 Fallkirk Frightened
Rabbit (James Scott) trailed the league in offense with 14 runs scored.
The 4-3 Winnetka Bahai allowed the most runs with 45.  And the Garmisch-
Partenkirchen Shebazzle! (Andrew Bernstein) had the worst run margin 
with -24.

STARTER OF THE WEEK:
Barcelona's PEDRO MARTINEZ saved 5.3 runs above average.  In his two 
starts, he threw 16 innings, allowing 2 runs on 9 hits, 5 walks and 
struck out 22.  Runners-up were Cincinnati's Shane Reynolds (5.1), 
Zzyzx's Chris Peters (4.7), Ferlin's Crappy Mark Clark (4.2) and 
Barcelona's Curt Schilling (4.0).

RELIEVER OF THE WEEK:
Baraboo's BILL SWIFT saved 3.1 runs above average, pitching 10 innings,
allowing 3 runs on 8 hits and 0 walks.  Runners-up were Winnetka's
LaTroy Hawkins (2.4), Winnetka's Rick Aguilera (2.3), McCullers Chris
Carpenter (2.2) and Tewksbury's Anthony Telford (2.0).

BAD PITCHER OF THE WEEK:

was Ferlin's Armando Benitez (-7.6).
