queens of the stone age league
weekly awards for the week ending may 10


GOOD TEAM OF THE WEEK:
The NEWFOUNDLAND PERFECTOS (John Sasman) and the CHINATRUST WHALES (Rob Miller) went
5-1 for the week.  Newfoundland scored 39 runs and allowed 18 runs, while Chinatrust
scored 35 and allowed 21.  The 5-2 Tarboro Goobers (Will Lingo) led the league in 
offense with 49 runs and in run margin at +24 runs.   Three teams tied for fewest
runs allowed with 18 -- Newfoundland, McCullers (John Manuel) and Felldownhead (Doug
English).

BAD TEAM OF THE WEEK:
The ZZYZX LOADIES (Matt Poole) were 0-7, scoring 22 runs and allowing 38 runs.   The
Ferlin Huskies (Jeff Chernow) and the Rocky Mountain Oysters (Haverty/Mojo) scored
the fewest runs (17).  The Fallkirk Frightened Rabbit (James Scott) allowed the most
runs (39).  The Ferlin Huskies had the poorest run margin of -21 runs.

STARTER OF THE WEEK:
Rocky Mountain's JEFF SUPPAN pitched two complete games, one a shutout, allowing 11 hits
and three walks while striking out nine.  He saved his team 6.2 runs above average. 
Runners-up were Newfoundland's Orlando Hernandez (5.4) and Felldownhead's Jamie Moyer (5.1).

RELIEVER OF THE WEEK:
Evanston's JERRY SPRADLIN saved 3.2 runs for his team, pitching 6.2 innings, allowing one
hit and one walk, striking out five.  Also with solid weeks out the bullpen were Ferdinand's
Danny Graves (3.1 RS) and Baraboo's CJ Nitkowski (3.0).

BAD PITCHER OF THE WEEK:
Garmisch-Partenkirchen's GREG SWINDELL cost his team 7.3 runs above average.  Also bad were
Okoboji's Frank Castillo (-7.0), Fallkirk's Kirt Ojala (-7.0) and Century's Gabe White (-6.9).
