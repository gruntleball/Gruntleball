GOOD TEAM OF THE WEEK:
The BARCELONA BREWERS (Al Marshall) went 5-1 for the week, scoring 38 
runs while allowing 29.  They were a bit lucky with a pythagorean win %
of .632 vs their .833 actual.  The 4-2 Winnetka Baha'i (Carroll/Kleine)
led the league with 46 runs scored.  The 4-2 Tewksbury Bobs (James
Bailey) and the 3-3 Okoboji Cropdusters each allowed only 19 runs.  The
4-2 Ferlin Huskies (Jeff Chernow) had a 15 run margin to lead in that
category.

BAD TEAM OF THE WEEK:
Both the Baraboo Goitermen (Mike Canter) and the FALLKIRK FRIGHTENED 
RABBIT (James Scott) went 1-5.  Fallkirk tied with the 2-4 McCullers
Lancers (John Manuel) for fewest runs scored with 17.  Baraboo allowed 
the most runs with 45, and the 2-4 Garmisch-Partenkirchen Shebazzle! 
(Andrew Bernstein) allowed 17 more runs than they scored.

STARTER OF THE WEEK:
Rocky Mountain's MIKE MUSSINA saved 4.0 runs above average, pitching 
12.1 innings, allowing 3 runs on 9 hits and 1 walk, striking out 10.

RELIEVER OF THE WEEK:
