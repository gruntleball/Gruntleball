GOOD TEAM OF THE WEEK:
The NEWFOUNDLAND PERFECTOS (John Sasman), Felldownhead Firsts (Doug English) and the
Barcelona Brewers (Al Marshall) were 6-1 for the week.  The 5-2 Dun Laoghaire Messiahs
led the league in scoring with 53 runs.  The Firsts led the league in run prevention
(really) with 17.  And the 5-2 Tewksbury Bobs (James Bailey) had the best run margin
at +22.

BAD TEAM OF THE WEEK:
The FERLIN HUSKIES (Jeff Chernow) were 0-7, scoring 36 runs and allowing 61.  The 1-6
Garmisch-Partenkirchen Chim-Chim Shebazzle! (Andrew Bernstein) scored the fewest runs
(17).  The Huskies allowed the most runs (61) and the Shebazzle! had the worst margin
of -31 runs.
