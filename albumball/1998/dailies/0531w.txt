GOOD TEAM OF THE WEEK:
The NEWFOUNDLAND PERFECTOS (John Sasman) went 6-0 for the week and
needs to just fucking stop it, leading the league in neither runs
scored, runs allowed, or margin.  The 4-2 Evanston Endorphins (Thom 
Henninger) led the league in scoring with 41 runs and allowed only 23
which resulted in their league leading margin of +18 runs.  The 5-2 
Rocky Mountain Oysters (Haverty/Mojo) tied Evanston in runs scored, but
did so in seven games.  The 4-2 Century Atoms (Randy Stearns) and the
4-2 McCullers Lancers (John Manuel) led the league in run prevention 
with 18.

BAD TEAM OF THE WEEK:
The FALLKIRK FRIGHTENED RABBIT went 0-6, scoring 28 runs while allowing
40 for a margin of -12.  The 1-6 Ferlin Huskies (Jeff Chernow) offense 
continues to lie dormant, scoring a mere 19 runs and allowing a league-
high 43 runs and thus having the poorest run margin as well.
