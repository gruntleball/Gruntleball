GOOD TEAM OF THE WEEK:
The TEWKSBURY BOBS (James Bailey) and the Tarboro Goobers (Will Lingo)
went 5-1.  The 4-2 Winnetka Baha'i led the league with 48 runs scored,
while the Bobs led in run prevention (10 runs allowed) and margin (+25).
runs scored and tied with the 5-2 Dun Laoghaire Messiahs (Ron Potter) 
in run prevention with 19.

BAD TEAM OF THE WEEK:
The FELLDOWNHEAD FIRSTS (Doug English) were 0-6, scoring 20 runs and
giving up 56 for a margin of -36.  The 1-5 Fallkirk Frightened Rabbit
also scored only 20 runs for the week.

STARTER OF THE WEEK:
Tewksbury's WILSON ALVAREZ saved 3.9 runs above average, with his 8-inning
start where he allowed one run, one hit and one walk and struck out six.
Runners-up were Tarboro's Brett Tomko (3.5), Tewksbury's Mike Thurman (3.5),
Barcelona's Curt Schilling (3.3) and Ferlin's Kirk Rueter (3.2).

RELIEVER OF THE WEEK:
Newfoundland's XAVIER HERNANDEZ saved 2.5 runs above average, pitching five
shutout innings, allowing one hit and one walk.  Also with noteworth weeks
were Ferdinand's Tom Gordon (2.5) and Jim Corsi (2.2), and Winnetka's Armando
Reynoso (2.2).

BAD PITCHER OF THE WEEK:
Felldownhead's RICK KRIVDA wins again!  He cost his team 15.2 runs compared
to an average pitcher.  The next worst was Century's Steve Reed (-8.0).
