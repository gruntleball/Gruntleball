GOOD TEAM OF THE WEEK:
The Dún Laoghaire Messiahs (Ron Potter) went 6-0 for the week, scoring 
a league- leading 52 runs and giving up only 16 for a league-best +36 
run margin.  Only the 5-1 Newfoundland Perfectos (John Sasman) gave up
fewer runs (13).

BAD TEAM OF THE WEEK:
Five teams went 1-5 for the week, but the FERDINAND MAGELLANS (Mat
Olkin) did it best, scoring 26 runs while allowing a league-high 52.
The 1-5 Okoboji Cropdusters (Bryan Smith) scored the fewest runs (15).
