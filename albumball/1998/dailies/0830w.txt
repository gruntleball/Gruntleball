GOOD TEAM OF THE WEEK:
The Dún Laoghaire Messiahs were 6-0, scoring 54 runs and allowing 34,
which was not tops in any run category.  The 3-3 Tewksbury Bobs scored
56 runs.  The 5-1 Ferlin Huskies allowed only 16 runs and had a league-
best 21 run margin.

BAD TEAM OF THE WEEK:
The Baraboo Goitermen went 0-6, scoring 22 runs and allowing a tied-
for-league-worst 48 runs, resulting in a -26 run margin.  The 2-4
Felldownhead Firsts trailed in offense with 17 runs scored.
