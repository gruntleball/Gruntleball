GOOD TEAM OF THE WEEK:
The NEWFOUNDLAND PERFECTOS and Winnetka Baha'i went 6-1 for the week.
Newfoundland scored 65 runs (best) and allowed 21 for a league-best
margin of +44 runs.  The 5-1 Evanston Endorphins allowed only 5 runs
for the WEEK, while scoring 19.

BAD TEAM OF THE WEEK:
Four teams were 1-5, led by the FERDINAND MAGELLANS who scored 18 runs
while allowing 48 for a -30 margin.  The Fallkirk Frightened Rabbit only
scored 11 runs, and the Garmisch-Partenkirchen Shebazzle! allowed 52.

STARTER OF THE WEEK:
Tarboro's BRIAN BOHANON saved 5.6 runs above average, pitching 15.2
innings, allowing 4 runs, 3 earned, on 7 hits and 5 walks.  Runners-up
were Rocky Mountain's Donovan Osborne (5.0), Barcelona's Bobby M. Jones
(4.5), Garmisch-Partenkirchen's Ramiro Mendoza and Zzyzx's Rick Helling 
(4.4).

RELIEVER OF THE WEEK:
Zzyzx's Mike Fetters saved 3.0 runs above average, pitching six scoreless
innings, allowing only a walk.  Runners-up were Tarboro's Bill Simas (2.0),
Felldownhead's Mike Stanton and McCullers Chris Carpenter (1.9).

BAD PITCHER OF THE WEEK:
Chinatrust's Doug Jones wins with 11.3 runs allowed worse than average, 
just two weeks after winning reliever of the week.
