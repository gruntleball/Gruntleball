queens of the stone age league
weekly awards for the week ending september 6


GOOD TEAM OF THE WEEK:
The NEWFOUNDLAND PERFECTOS and Okoboji Cropdusters went 6-0, with
Newfoundland's run margin being slightly better at 22 runs compared
to 18 for Okoboji.  The 5-1 Dun Laoghaire Messiahs led the league in 
offense with 39 runs scored.  The 5-1 Barcelona Brewers led the league
in run prevention with 14 scored against.

BAD TEAM OF THE WEEK:
The BARABOO GOITERMEN, Cincinnati Reds and Fallkirk Frightened Rabbit
each went 0-6.  Baraboo scored only 9 runs all week, lowest in the 
league and had the poorest run margin of -31.  The Zzyzx Loadies gave
up the most runs with 49.

STARTER OF THE WEEK:
Barcelona's PEDRO MARTINEZ saved 8.0 runs above average, allowing 1 run
on 4 hits plus 1 walk in 17.0 innings, striking out 22.  The next best
was Evanston's Omar Daal and Chinatrust's Randy Johnson with 3.6 runs 
saved, followed by Okoboji's Todd Van Poppel with 3.5.

RELIEVER OF THE WEEK:
Newfoundland's Robb Nen and Winnetka's Alan Levine saved 2.6 runs above
average, though Nen was far more effective.

BAD PITCHER OF THE WEEK:
Chinatrust's DOUG JONES cost his team 8.7 runs above in 2/3 of an inning.
Following him was teammate Shane Reynolds who allowed 7.2 runs above 
average.
