GOOD TEAM OF THE WEEK:
The NEWFOUNDLAND PERFECTOS (John Sasman) went 6-1, scoring a league-
leading 50 runs and allowing 28 for a margin of +22.  The 4-3 Winnetka 
Baha'i (Carroll/Kleine) allowed only 21 runs.  The 5-2 Barcelona 
Brewers (Al Marshall) had the highest margin at +24.

BAD TEAM OF THE WEEK:
Both the BARABOO GOITERMEN (Mike Canter) and the Felldownhead Firsts
(Doug English) went 1-6.  Baraboo scored 21 runs and allowed 38 for a
-17 run margin.  The 2-5 Fallkirk Frightened Rabbit (James Scott) only
scored 16 runs.  The 4-3 Zzyzx Loadies (Matt Poole) allowed 51 runs
while scoring only 38.

STARTER OF THE WEEK:
Winnetka's CHAN HO PARK saved his team 6.7 runs above average, throwing 
18 shutout innings on 12 hits and two walks, striking out 15.  Runners-
up were McCullers' Kenny Rogers (5.5) and Kerry Wood (4.8), Century's
Francisco Cordova (4.4) and Newfoundland's Ramon Martinez (4.0)

RELIEVER OF THE WEEK:
