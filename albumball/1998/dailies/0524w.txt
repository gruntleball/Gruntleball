STARTER OF THE WEEK:
Dun Laoghaire's GREG MADDUX pitched 16.0 innings, allowing only one run
on 7 hits and a walk, striking out 11, saving his team 6.8 runs for the
week.  Runners up were Chinatrust's Jose Lima (4.9), Tewksbury's Rick
Reed (4.7) and Century's Gabe White (4.2).

RELIEVER OF THE WEEK:
Rocky Mountain's WAYNE GOMES pitched 5.0 innings in four game, allowing 
one hit and no walks, striking out five, and saving his team 2.4 runs.
Runners-up were Barcelona's Mike Timlin (2.2), Baraboo's Mike Buddie 
(2.2) and Ferlin's Mike Cather (2.0).

BAD PITCHER OF THE WEEK:

innings in two starts, allowing 13 runs on 17 hits, 4 walks, but only 
one homerun.  Also back to the sucky side was Rocky Mountain's Jeff
Suppan (-9.5) and Tarboro's Amaury Telemaco (-8.3) in just one inning!
