GOOD TEAM OF THE WEEK:
The NEWFOUNDLAND PERFECTOS, Cincinnati Reds and Dún Laoghaire Messiahs 
went 5-1 for the week.  Dún Laoghaire led the league in offense with
48 runs scored, while the 4-2 Evanston Endorphins and Newfoundland led
in run prevention with 19 runs allowed.  Newfoundland also had the best
run margin at +25.

BAD TEAM OF THE WEEK:
The FALLKIRK FRIGHTENED RABBIT went 0-6, scoring 17 runs and allowing
49, both tied for worst in the league with the 2-4 Rocky Mountain Oysters
and the 2-4 Ferdinand Magellans, respectively.

STARTER OF THE WEEK:
Newfoundland's RAMON MARTINEZ saved 5.6 runs above average, pitching 16.2
innings, allowing 3 runs on 9 hits, 8 walks and striking out 17.  Runners-
up were Winnetka's Darryl Kile (4.4), Chinatrust's Randy Johnson(4.4),
Evanston's White Lines Gooden (4.3) and Ferlin's Masato Yoshii (4.3).

RELIEVER OF THE WEEK:

over 3 games, allowing 1 run on 3 hits and no walks.  Runners-up were

Swindell (2.1), McCullers' Russ Springer, Ferlin's Armando Benitez and
