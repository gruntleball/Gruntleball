queens of the stone age league
weekly awards for the week ending july 12


GOOD TEAM OF THE WEEK:
The TARBORO GOOBERS (Will Lingo) were 3-1 for the week, but so were a 
lot of teams.  They tied the 3-1 Zzyzx Loadies (Matt Poole) for run 
margin, but had better run prevention and a slightly better Pythag-
orean winning percentage.  Zzyzx led the league in offense with 35
runs scored.  The 3-1 Okoboji Cropdusters (Bryan Smith) led the league
in run prevention, allowing only 13.

BAD TEAM OF THE WEEK:
The GARMISCH-PARTENKIRCHEN SHEBAZZLE! (Andrew Bernstein) and the 
Ferdinand Magellans (Mat Olkin) were both 0-4.  The Shebazzle! had the 
poorest run margin of -17.  The 1-3 Cincinnati Reds (Chuck Miller) 
scored only 9 runs to trail in offense and the 2-2 Ferlin Huskies
(Jeff Chernow) allowed 35 runs, most in the league.

STARTER OF THE WEEK:
Chinatrust's STEVE COOKE saved his team 4.3 runs above average with
a complete game shutout.  Zzyzx's Rick Helling saved 4.2 runs.  Runners-
up were Cincinnati's David Wells and Livan Hernandez (3.3 and 3.0), 
respectively, and Newfoundland's Chuck Finley (3.0).

RELIEVER OF THE WEEK:
Century's RICK WHITE saved 2.3 runs above average.  Willie Banks of 
Barcelona saved 1.8 runs and Evanston's Jerry Spradlin saved 1.7 runs.

BAD PITCHER OF THE WEEK:
Century's BRAD RADKE cost his team 8.8 runs above average.  Runners-up
were Dun Laoghaire's David Cone (-8.6), Tewksbury's Keith Foulke (-8.1),
probably the curse of the All-Star game.
