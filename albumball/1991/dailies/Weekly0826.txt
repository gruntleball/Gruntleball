TLM: Weekly Awards for August 26th - September 1st

Good Team of the Week
The Barcelona Brewers were a perfect 5-0 for the week and have won ten
straight.  The Effin Shebazzle had the largest run margin at +20 over
five games.

Bad Team of the Week
The Houston Streets went 1-5 and were outscored 29 to 17.

Hitter of the Week
Effin's Jeff Treadway created 10.1 runs (in just 18 plate appearances) 
while putting up a 1.651 OPS during the week (643/722/929).

Bad Hitter of the Week
Tewksbury's Gary Gaetti managed 0.0 runs created and a .100 OPS in 20
plate appearances.

Starter of the Week
Century's Ron Darling pitched a complete game shutout in which he allowed
six hits and no walks while striking out nine.

Reliever of the Week
For the second straight week, a Reykjavik reliever gets the nod.  Joe
Boever appeared in four games, pitching 5.2 innings and allowed only
two hits, no walks, and struck out seven.

Bad pitcher of the Week 
Nederland's Joey Table appeared in 5 games, tossed four innings of 
batting practice and allowed 11 hits and three walks for a total of 
eight runs.
