TLM: Weekly Awards for July 22nd - 28th

Good Team of the Week
The Chinatrust Whales were 5-0 for the week.  Both the Whales and
the Ferdinand Magellans outscored their opponents by 28 runs.

Bad Team of the Week
The Needmore Perfectos were 0-4 for the week, outscored 15 to 27.

Hitter of the Week
Frank Thomas of Chinatrust created 9.8 runs in 21 plate appearances.

Bad Hitter of the Week
Lou Whitaker had a rough week, putting up just a .214 OPS in 14 plate
appearances.

Starter of the Week
Effin's Nolan Ryan pitched a complete game shutout, allowing just 2
hits and walking 3, while striking out 11.

Reliever of the Week
Ferdinand's Melido Perez pitched 7.2 innings of scoreless relief, 
allowing just 2 hits and 3 walks.

Bad pitcher of the Week 
Evanston's Oil Can Boyd gave up 11 runs over two starts spanning 
8.2 innings.
