TLM: 4/29 Weekly Awards 

Good Team of the Week - The Effin Shebazzle! get the nod for
the 3rd straight week, going 6-1 and outscoring the opposition
38-24.  The Butt Mountain Beavis had the greatest run margin at
+22 runs.

Bad Team of the Week - Lots of teams went 2-5, but Nederland did
it in grand style, being outscored 55-27.

Hitter of the Week - For the 2nd straight week, Cincinnati's 
Barry Larkin leads the league in runs created.  He created 12.8
runs with a 1.845 OPS with 5 home runs.  Runner up was Chinatrust's
Milt Thompson with 8.7 RC.

Un-Hitter of the Week - Reykjavik's Mike Bordick put up a nifty
.203 OPS over 26 plate appearances.

Starter of the Week - Normally I give this to a pitcher who pitched
a shutout, but Pete Harnisch was dominating, allowing 2 hits and a
walk for just 1 run.  He struck out 11.

Reliever of the Week - Chinatrust's Bill Swift and Scott Radinsky
tossed a combined 10.2 innings, allowing 6 hits and 2 walks, and
striking out 9.  Not surprisingly they allowed no runs.

Un-pitcher of the Week - Needmore's Chuck Finley appeared in two 
games but lasted only 5 innings, in which he allowed 11 hits 
(including 4 homeruns) and 4 walks which resulted in 13 runs.
