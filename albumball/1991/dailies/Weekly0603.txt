TLM: Weekly Awards for May 27

Good Team of the Week
The Butt Mountain Beavis went 5-1 for the week with a 35-27 run
margin.  Tewksbury and Cincinnati each had better run margins at
+10 runs.

Bad Team of the Week
Both the Century Atoms and the Reykjavik 101ers were 1-6 during 
the week.  Reykjavik was the slightly worse of the two, allowing 
15 more runs than they scored.

Hitter of the Week
Needmore's Danny Tartabull created 13.6 runs and outpaced the field.
Butt Mountain's Andre Dawson created 8.0 runs for runner up, and did
so in just 9 plate appearances with a 2.500 OPS.

Bad Hitter of the Week
Toronto's Cecil Fielder had a rough week, sporting a 053/100/053 line
over 20 plate appearances that included 7 strikeouts.

Starter of the Week
Grain Belt's Frank Castillo tossed 7 shutout innings in which he allowed
just 4 batters to reach base.  No runs were scored.

Reliever of the Week
Baraboo's Scott Scudder appeared in 3 games and threw 6.1 innings of
scoreless relief, allowing just a single walk.

Bad pitcher of the Week 
11 runs in 3 innings... it's gotta be Nederland's Paul Gibson.
