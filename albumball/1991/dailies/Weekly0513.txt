TLM: 05/13 Weekly Awards 

Good Team of the Week
The Chinatrust Whales went 3-1 for the week (.750) and both
Dun Laoghaire and Cincinnati went 5-2 (.714).  Chinatrust had
the larger run margin per game at +3.25 runs.

Bad Team of the Week
Both the Baraboo Goitermen and the Butt Mountain Beavis were
1-5 during the week, the latter with a run margin of only -2.
Baraboo wins!

Hitter of the Week
Dun Laoghaire's Mickey Tettleton created 11.4 runs in just 17
plate appearances (43.8 RC27), with a 1.933 OPS.  Albert Belle
was runner-up with 9.1 RC.

Bad Hitter of the Week
Cincinnati's Ken Caminiti created just 0.1 runs over 25 plate
appearances with a .122 OPS.

Starter of the Week
Beavis' Roger Clemens's complete game 2-hitter gets the nod.
He walked no one and struck out 9.

Reliever of the Week
Barcelona's Rick Aguilera appeared in 4 games and pitched 5.0
innings, allowing 1 hit and 1 walk while striking out 6.

Bad pitcher of the Week 
Nederland's Tony Castillo made 2 starts totaling 9 innings in
which he allowed 9 runs and 23 baserunners.
