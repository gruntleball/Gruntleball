TLM: Weekly Awards for May 27

Good Team of the Week
The Toronto Tools Police went 6-1 during the week and outscored their
opponents 38 to 22.  Both the 5-1 Whales and the 5-2 Endorphins had 
higher run margins.

Bad Team of the Week
The Century Atoms were 1-6 during the week and were outscored 35-16.

Hitter of the Week
Butt Mountain's Dave Martinez created 11.0 runs for the week whilst
compiling a 1.321 OPS over 26 plate appearances.  He barely surpassed
Needmore's Danny Tartabull who created 10.9 runs.

Bad Hitter of the Week
Needmore's Gary Sheffield created 0.2 runs with a .220 OPS in 19 plate
appearances.

Starter of the Week
Reykjavik's Alex Fernandez pitched a complete game shutout, allowing
just 3 hits and 3 walks, while striking out 8.  He and Mike Mussina 
were similarly masterful, with Mussina going 9 5 0 0 2 7 for the week.

Reliever of the Week
Chinatrust's Scott Radinsky appeared in 4 games, pitching 4.1 innings
and allowing one hit and no walks while striking out 5.

Bad pitcher of the Week 
Dun Laoghaire's Tom Glavine gave up 12 runs in 8.2 innings, in which
he surrendered 14 hits (including 2 HR) and 3 walks.
