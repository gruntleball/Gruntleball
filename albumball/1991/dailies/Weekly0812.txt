TLM: Weekly Awards for August 12th - 18th

Good Team of the Week
The Dun Laoghaire Messiahs went 6-1 and outscored their opponents
40 to 17.  On a per game basis, Effin was the most dominant with
a 22-run margin in just 6 games.

Bad Team of the Week
The Evanston Endorphins were winless with 6 defeats and were outscored
by 16 runs.  While their results weren't good, Reykjavik was more
feeble, being outscored 46-13 but managing to win 2 games.

Hitter of the Week
Houston's Kirby Puckett had a pretty big week, creating 13.6 runs in
just 5 games while putting up a 1.682 OPS and hitting 4 homeruns.  He
just edged out Chinatrust's Keith Miller's 13.0 runs created.

Bad Hitter of the Week
Reykjavik's Scott Bradley managed a .176 OPS for the week with no hits
and 3 walks.

Starter of the Week
Effin's Juan Guzman allowed 4 hits and 3 walks in his only start, while
striking out 7.  Needmore's Tim Belcher gets runner-up.

Reliever of the Week
Needmore's Joel Johnston appeared in 4 games and threw 5.1 scoreless
innings during which he allowed 2 hits, no walks and struck out 6.

Bad pitcher of the Week 
Nederland's Kevin Morton pitched 11 innings in 3 games (2 starts)
and allowed 20 hits, including 3 homeruns, and 4 walks which resulted
in 13 runs allowed.
