TLM: Weekly Awards for August 5th - 11th

Good Team of the Week
The Effin Shebazzle went 5-0 for the week, and lead the league by
outscoring their opponents by 26 runs, 35-9.

Bad Team of the Week
The Nederland Frozen Dead Guys were 0-5 for the week and had the
2nd worst run margin at -18, bested by Tewksbury at -19.

Hitter of the Week
Barcelona's Tony Phillips created 9.0 runs in 7 starts and 29 plate
appearances.  For the week, he hit 346/414/808.

Bad Hitter of the Week
Cincinnati's Rob Deer managed just a .163 OPS in 25 plate appearances.

Starter of the Week
Chinatrust's Kevin Tapani made two starts, one of them a complete game
shutout.  In 17 innings, he allowed 9 hits and a walk, striking out 12,
and gave up just one run.

Reliever of the Week
Grain Belt's Dan Petry appeared in 5 games, pitching 10 innings, during
which he allowed NO runs, 3 hits and 1 walk, while striking out 6.

Bad pitcher of the Week 
Ferlin's John Kiely allowed 8 runs on 8 hits and 2 walks in just ONE
inning of work.  That's impressive.
