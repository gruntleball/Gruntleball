TLM: Weekly Awards for June 10-16

Good Team of the Week
The Butt Mountain Beavis went 6-0 for the week with a 40-15 run
margin--wow!

Bad Team of the Week
The Reykjavik 101ers were 0-7 for the week, and were outscored by
31 runs, by far the worst in the league.

Hitter of the Week
Butt Mountain's Ryne Sandberg created 16.5 runs in 27 plate appearances.
He compiled an OPS of 1.801 for the week.  Paul Molitor created 14.8 runs 
as runner-up, which would've won it for him just about any other week.

Bad Hitter of the Week
Reyjavik's Sammy Sosa created -0.1 runs in 20 plate appearances with a
.100 OPS.

Starter of the Week
Effin's Jack McDowell pitched a complete game shutout in which he 
gave up just 1 hit and 3 walks, while striking out 13.

Reliever of the Week
Barcelona's Lee Smith appeared in 5 games and pitched 4.2 innings of
shutout ball, in whcih he allowed just 2 hits, no walks, and struck
out 8.  Rob Dibble struck out 12 in just 5.1 innings, and also allowed
no runs, but Lee Smith gets the nod.

Bad pitcher of the Week 
Toronto's Ramon Martinez gave up 11 runs on 14 hits and 2 walks in 
just 5.1 innings over two starts.
