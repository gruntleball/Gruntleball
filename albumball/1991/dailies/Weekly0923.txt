TLM: Weekly Awards for the Week of September 23rd (through September 30th)

Good Team of the Week
The Cincinnati Reds went 6-1 in the final week of the season, outscoring
their opponents 29-19.  The 6-2 Chinatrust Whales had the greatest run 
margin at +22 runs.

Bad Team of the Week
The Grain Belt Premiums went 1-7 and were outscored 50-34.  The poorest
run margin was 19-41 by Evanston.

Hitter of the Week
Ferlin's Ellis Burks created 12.4 runs in 29 plate appearances and put
up a 1.392 OPS for the week.  Runners up were Chinatrust's Larry Walker 
(11.4) and Reykjavik's Robin Young (10.2).

Bad Hitter of the Week
Needmore's Mitch Webster put up a .118 OPS in 17 plate appearances.	

Starter of the Week
Chinatrust's Luis Aquino pitched a complete game, two-hit shutout, in 
which he walked none and struck out three.

Reliever of the Week
Chinatrust's Rob Dibble appeared in three games, tossoing 5.1 no-hit
innings, walking three and striking out 12.

Bad pitcher of the Week 
Reykjaviks's Jimmy Jones made two starts, totaling 8.1 innings.  He gave
up 12 hits, including FOUR homeruns, and six walks.  He also fanned 12.
