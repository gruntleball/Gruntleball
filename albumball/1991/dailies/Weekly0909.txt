TLM: Weekly Awards for September 9th - September 15th

Good Team of the Week
The Dun Laoghaire Messiahs were 7-0 for the week and outscored their
opponents by nearly 5 1/2 runs per game.

Bad Team of the Week
Both the Baraboo Goitermen and Grain Belt Premiums were 0-7, but Grain
Belt was outscored by 30 runs.  Toronto (2-5) had the worst run margin
at -32 runs.

Hitter of the Week
Needmore's Danny Tartabull had the best week of the year, creating 16.9
runs in 28 plate appearances, hitting 500/536/1192.

Bad Hitter of the Week
Chinatrust's Paul O'Neill created 0.1 runs with a .153 OPS during the
week.  

Starter of the Week
Butt Mountain's Roger Clemens pitched a complete game 3-hitter, allowing
no walks and striking out six.

Reliever of the Week
Tewksbury's Barry Jones pitched six innings of relief over five games, and
allowed just 2 hits and a walk.

Bad pitcher of the Week 
Grain Belt's Jeff Ballard allowed 12 runs on 9 hits and 6 walks in just 5.2
innings.
