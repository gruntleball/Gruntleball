TLM: Weekly Awards for May 20

Good Team of the Week
The Chinatrust Whales went 5-0 for the week, and outscored 
their opponenst 36-12.  The Houston Streets were 6-1 for the
week.

Bad Team of the Week
The Baraboo Goitermen went 1-5 during the week, though they 
were only outscored by 5 runs.  In contrast, the 1-4 Ferlin
Huskies were outscored 31-9.

Hitter of the Week
Houston's Roberto Alomar created 12.4 runs for the week, 
putting up a 1.346 OPS over 35 plate appearances.

Bad Hitter of the Week
Bobby Bonilla of Grain Belt managed just a .197 OPS in 26 plate
appearances.

Starter of the Week
ChinaTrust's Kevin Tapani made two starts and threw 17 innings,
over which he allowed ZERO runs, 6 hits, 3 walks, and struck out
7.

Reliever of the Week
Toronto's Jeff Innis made 4 appearances totaling 5.1 innings.  He
allowed 2 hits and a walk, while striking out 5.  And of course,
no runs.

Bad pitcher of the Week 
Dun Laoghaire's Todd Stottlemyre yielded 10 runs in 6 innings over
two starts.  He allowed 11 hits and 7 walks for a nifty WHIP of 3.0!
