TLM: Weekly Awards for July 1st - July 7th

Marquis Grissom runs wild!!!

Good Team of the Week
The Dun Laoghaire Messiahs were 6-0, and held their opponents to
just SIX runs for the week!  Ferlin went 6-1 and scored 58 runs. Wow.

Bad Team of the Week
Three teams went 1-6, but Nederland was worst, yielding 59 runs and 
were outscored by 43.  Good job Will!

Hitter of the Week
Ferlin's Todd Zeile created 13.9 runs for the week with an OPS of 1.302
over 35 plate appearances.  Runner up was Ozzie Smith with 11.9!

Bad Hitter of the Week
Nederland's Glenn Braggs put up a .211 OPS in 19 plate appearances.	

Starter of the Week
Three Dun Laoghaire starters gave up zero runs:  Abbott, Candiotti and
Mercker.  Tom Candiotti was the most effective, allowing just 5 batters
to reach, and struck out 10.

Reliever of the Week
Cincinnati's Mike Stanton allowed just one hit and one walk over 4.1 
innings spanning 3 games.  He allowed no runs and struck out 4.

Bad pitcher of the Week 
Juan Agosto of Nederland gave up 16 runs in just 6.2 innings.  Jeff
Johnson, also of Nederland gave up 11 runs in just 4 innings.
