TLM: Weekly Awards for August 19th - 25th

Good Team of the Week
The Houston Streets and Dun Laoghaire Messiahs each went 6-1.  Houston
outscored their opponents 37 to 13, compared to 44 to 23 for Dun 
Laoghaire.

Bad Team of the Week
The Baraboo Goitermen went 1-6 and were outscored by 27 runs.

Hitter of the Week
Cincinnati's Barry Larkin created 14.1 runs while compiling a 1.582 OPS
during the week.

Bad Hitter of the Week
Nederland's Shawon Dunston created 0.0 runs along with a 0.91 OPS.

Starter of the Week
Dun Laoghaire's Tom Edens pitched nine innings of shutout ball, and
allowed just one hit and two walks.  Among starters, Nederland's 
Allan Anderson pitched 11 scoreless innings over two starts but 
allowed nine baserunners.

Reliever of the Week
Reykjavik's Mike Hartley appeared in two games and threw 5.1 scoreless
innings during which he allowed three hits and no walks.

Bad pitcher of the Week 
Evanston's Wade Taylor made two starts but lasted just 5.0 innings,
probably because he allowed 10 runs on 10 hits and five walks.
