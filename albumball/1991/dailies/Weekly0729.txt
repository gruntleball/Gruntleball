TLM: Weekly Awards for July 29th - August 4th

Good Team of the Week
The Chinatrust Whales were 6-1 for the week. The Dun Laoghaire 
Messiahs were the most dominant, outscoring their opponents 47-15
over 6 games.

Bad Team of the Week
The Nederland Frozen Dead Guys were 1-4 for the week, as were Grain
Belt and Needmore, but Nederland was outscored by 28 runs.

Hitter of the Week
Butt Mountain's Ryne Sandberg created 11.5 runs in 25 plate 
appearances with a 1.560 OPS.

Bad Hitter of the Week
Ferlin's Spike Owen put up a .111 OPS in 18 plate appearances.  So
much for a change of scenery.

Starter of the Week
Century's Greg Hibbard pitched a complete game shutout, allowing just
3 hits and no walks.

Reliever of the Week
Ferdinand's Bruce Ruffin was just dominant, pitching 9 innings of 
relief, giving up 2 hits, 4 walks and striking out 8.

Bad pitcher of the Week 
Needmore's Joel Johnston allowed 8 runs on 5 hits and 4 walks in 
just 2.2 innings.
