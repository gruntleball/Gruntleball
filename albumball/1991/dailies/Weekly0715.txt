TLM: Weekly Awards for July 15th - 21st

Good Team of the Week
The Barcelona Brewers went 6-1, scoring 38 runs and allowing just 21.

Bad Team of the Week
The Ferlin Huskies went 0-6 and were outscored 50-26.

Hitter of the Week
Chili Davis of Barcelona created 11.7 runs in 33 plate appearances,
hitting 429/515/929 for the week with 4 homeruns.

Bad Hitter of the Week
Century's Leo Gomez started 5 games and in 21 plate appearances
managed a lofty .250 OPS.

Starter of the Week
Out of many worthy candidates, Toronto's David Cone gets the nod, as he
pitched an 8 innings shutout, striking out 7 and allowing just one hit
and one walk.

Reliever of the Week
Toronto's Tom Henke pitched in 4 games, totaling 4.2 innings, and was 
perfect, while striking out 6.

Bad pitcher of the Week 
Nederland's Paul Gibson gave up 12 runs in 8.2 innings over two starts.
In those 8.2 innings, he allowed 13 hits and 8 walks.
