Good Team of the Week - The Newfoundland Perfectos went 
6-1 for the week and outscored their opponents 48-26.

went 2-5 for the week.  Century was outscored by the 
largest margin of 11 runs.

runs during the week with an OPS of 1.586, hitting a pair
of homeruns and 4 doubles.

just 0.3 runs, with an OPS of .198 in 32 plate appearances.

Starter of the Week - Luis Aquino of Chinatrust gave up no
runs and just 4 baserunners over 9 innings.

Reliever of the Week - Mike Stanton of Cincinnati allowed 
just 2 baserunners over 5.2 innings spanning 4 games.

Un-pitcher of the Week - Grain Belt's Bob Welch gave up 12
runs in just 6 innings.
