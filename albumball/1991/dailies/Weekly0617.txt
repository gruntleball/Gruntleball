Good Team of the Week
The Ferdinand Magellans went 6-1 and outscored their opponents 40 
to 17.

Bad Team of the Week

Hitter of the Week
ChinaTrust's Frank Thomas created 12.3 runs in 29 plate appearances.
For the week, his OPS was 1.436.

Bad Hitter of the Week

one walk to show for his week.

Starter of the Week
Ferdinand's Tommy Greene won both of his starts, pitching 13.2 innings
and allowing no runs, 6 hits, 2 walks and struck out 11.

Reliever of the Week
Reykjavik's Rick Sutcliffe appeared in 5 games, tossed 7.0 innings of
scoreless relief, and was helped by 3 double plays.

Bad pitcher of the Week 
Beavis Roger Clemens yielded 11 runs in just 5 innings of work spanning
two starts.  Thirteen baserunners reached in those 5 innings.
