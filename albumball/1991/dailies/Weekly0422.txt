TLM: 4/22 Weekly Awards 

Good Team of the Week - The Effin Shebazzle! get the nod for
the 2nd straight week, going 6-1 and outscoring the opposition
45-15.  The Chinatrust Whales were also 6-1 for the week, but
were far less dominating.

Bad Team of the Week - The Houston Streets were just 1-4, and
were outscored 23-16.

Hitter of the Week - Cincinnati's Barry Larkin created an
amazing 15.4 runs for the week, compiling a 1.548 OPS with
4 home runs.  The runner-up, Dave Hollins had 8.9 RC.

Un-Hitter of the Week - Century's Leo Gomez created 0.1 runs with
a .148 OPS.	

Starter of the Week - Tewksbury's Kevin Gross tossed 13 scoreless
innings in 2 starts, striking out 13.

Reliever of the Week - Barcelona's Jeff Montgomery appeared in 5
games and threw six shutout innings, striking out 6.

Un-pitcher of the Week - Barcelona's John Smiley appeared in 4 games,
started 1 and gave up 10 runs on 15 hits with 3 walks in 7.0 innings.
