TLM: Weekly Awards for September 2nd - September 8th

Good Team of the Week
The Needmore Perfectos were 5-0, though only outscored their opponents
by 11 runs.  The Chinatrust Whales had the largest run margin at 20
runs.

Bad Team of the Week
The Ferlin Huskies went 1-5 and were outscored 33 to 16.  Nederland had
the worst run margin, though they were 2-4, at -23 runs.

Hitter of the Week
Ferdinand's Barry Bonds makes his first appearance of the year as player
of the week.  He put up a 1.321 OPS and created 12.1 runs in 28 plate
appearances.

Bad Hitter of the Week
Grain Belt's Ron Gant managed just a .222 OPS in 18 plate appearances.

Starter of the Week
Tewksbury's Steve Avery's one-hitter, where he faced the minimum 27 
batters, gets the nod for performance of the week, eclipsing Kevin
Tapani's 3-hit, no walk outing.

Reliever of the Week
Tough choices this week as no one really stands out.  Houston's Pat Rice
appeared in 3 games, throwing 3.1 innings and allowing just one hit.

Bad pitcher of the Week 
Nederland's Darryl Kile allowed 12 runs in just 3.1 innings.
