runs.  Dun Laoghaire had the largest margin of 18 runs.

Hitter of the Week
Hey, it's NOT Barry Larkin!  Toronto's Jose Canseco eeked 
out the award with 10.6 runs created -- a margin of 0.1 runs

Starter of the Week
Jack Morris tossed 17 innings of one-run ball for Chinatrust
over the course of the week.  He allowed 13 hits and 5 walks
while striking out 17.

Reliever of the Week
Ferdinand's Melido Perez appeared in 5 games and threw 7 
innings of shutout ball.  He allowed NO hits, 3 walks and 
struck out 8.

Bad pitcher of the Week

by allowing 18 baserunners and 2 homeruns.
