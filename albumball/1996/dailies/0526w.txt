WEEKLY AWARDS FOR WEEK ENDING MAY 26TH

GOOD TEAM OF THE WEEK:
The BUTT MOUNTAIN BEAVIS, Okoboji Farmers and Ferdinand Magellans each went 5-1.
The Winnetka Baha'i led the league in runs scored with 55.  Beavis led the league
in runs prevented, allowing only 16, and had the best run margin with a +33.

BAD TEAM OF THE WEEK:
The FELLDOWNHEAD FIRSTS and the Garmisch-Partenkirchen Chim Chim Shebazzle! were 
both 1-5.  The Rocky Mountain Oysters trailed the league in runs scored with 19,
and the Firsts allowed the most runs with 56.  They also had the poorest run 
margin at -23.

HITTER OF THE WEEK: 
Okoboji's DEVON WHITE had a huge week, creating 18.2 runs in 28 plate appearances
with a batting line of 565/643/1174.  Also putting up solid weeks were Winnetka's
Jeff Conine (14.3) and Frank Thomas (11.5).  Tarboro's Marvin Benard (of all people)
created 10.3 runs.

BAD HITTER OF THE WEEK:
Zzyzx's SHAWN GREEN hit 095/174/095 for the week in 23 plate appearances.

STARTER OF THE WEEK:
Butt Mountain's ROGER CLEMENS pitches a complete game, striking out 12.

RELIEVER OF THE WEEK:
Ferlin's ROD BECK pithces 6 shutout innings, walking one and striking out six.

BAD PITCHER OF THE WEEK:
On a per inning basis, he wasn't the worst, but Evanston's SCOTT SANDERS gave up
what has to be a record 21 runs in two games.  In 13.1 innings, he allowed 26 hits,
4 of them homeruns, walked four and struck out nine.  Jeff Fassero, in just 6.1 
innings, allowed 13 runs on 16 hits, SIX of them homeruns, walking three.
