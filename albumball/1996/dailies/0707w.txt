WEEKLY AWARDS FOR WEEK ENDING JULY 7TH

GOOD TEAM OF THE WEEK:
Four teams went 6-1 this week, led by the San Carlos Quentins who scored 59 runs and
allowed 30.  Also going 6-1 were the Chinatrust Whales 48-22, Okoboji Farmer 48-33 
and the Butt Mountain Beavis 30-22.  San Carlos led the league in scoring and margin.
The Whales and Beavis tied for fewest runs allowed with 22.

BAD TEAM OF THE WEEK:
The Century Atoms went 0-7, scoring 25 and allowing 53 for a -28 margin.  The Dun
Laoghaire Messiahs allowed more runs (63) and the Tewksbury Bobs scored the fewest
(20).

HITTER OF THE WEEK: 
Zzyzx's SAMMY SOSA created 14.0 runs in 28 plate appearances, batting 400/464/1280
with SEVEN homeruns.  Close behind were San Carlos's Roberto Alomar (13.4), Ferlin's
Ellis Burks (11.1), Evanston's Raul Mondesi (10.5) and Chinatrust's Jeff Conine (10.4).

BAD HITTER OF THE WEEK:
Tewksbury's John Flaherty batted 091/167/091 in 27 plate appearances.

STARTER OF THE WEEK:
Chinatrust's ROGER PAVLIK pitched 14.2 innings, allowing 3 runs on 8 hits and 7 walks
while striking out 14 and allowing no homeruns.

RELIEVER OF THE WEEK:
Barcelona's Paul Spoljaric thre 5.2 no-hit, no-run innings, walking two and striking 
out six.

BAD PITCHER OF THE WEEK:
Tewksbury's Chris Peters threw 3.2 innings in one game, giving up 15 runs on 14 hits 
including two homeruns, plus 9 walks.
