WEEKLY AWARDS FOR WEEK ENDING APRIL 14TH

GOOD TEAM OF THE WEEK:
Four teams went 6-1 during the week.  The best of these was the Newfoundland
Perfectos who scored 38 runs and allowed 18 for a margin of +20.  Also 6-1 
were the Chinatrust Whales, Butt Mountain Beavis and San Carlos Quentins. The
Ferlin Huskies led the league with 50 runs scored, and the Perfectos allowed
only 18 runs.

BAD TEAM OF THE WEEK:
Three teams were 1-6 for the week, the worst of these being the Garmisch-
Partenkirchen Chim-Chim Shebazzle! which scored 20 runs and allowed 55 for a
gawdawful -35 margin.  The Ferdinand Magellans and the Felldownhead Firsts
also suffered with margins of -23 and -18, respectively.  Felldownhead had
the least potent offense (17 runs) while Ferdinand and GPCC tied for most
generous pitching and defense with 55 runs allowed.

HITTER OF THE WEEK:
Ferdinand's Steve Finley created 15.0 runs in 27 plate appearances with a 
batting line of 560/593/1000.  Also among the double-digit RC producers were
San Carlos's Roberto Alomar (11.6), Rocky Mountain's Eric Karros (10.7) and
Ferlin's Ellis Burks (10.6).

BAD HITTER OF THE WEEK:
Zzyzx's Jay Bell created 0.1 runs in 19 plate appearances with a batting line
of 056/105/056.

STARTER OF THE WEEK:
Ferdinand's Huck Flener made two starts, pitching 12.0 innings, allowing 6 hits
and 1 walk, striking out five.

RELIEVER OF THE WEEK:
Chinatrust's Randy Johnson pitched 7.0 scoreless innings, allowing three hits, no
walks and struck out 14.  He also got 4 saves for what that's worth.

BAD PITCHER OF THE WEEK:
Ferdinand's (gad's they're all over the place) Tom Gordon made two starts, throwing
9.2 innings.  He allowed 15 runs on 22 hits plus six walks (no home runs though).
