WEEKLY AWARDS FOR WEEK ENDING MAY 12TH

GOOD TEAM OF THE WEEK:
The NEWFOUNDLAND PERFECTOS, Chinatrust Whales and Cincinnati Reds each went
6-1.  Chinatrust led the league in runs scored with 58; The 4-3 Barcelona 
Brewers allowed the fewest runs with 21, and the Perfectos had the greatest
run margin with +33 runs.

BAD TEAM OF THE WEEK:
The ROCKY MOUNTAIN OYSTERS were 1-6 and were in a 3-way tie for least runs
scored with 19, along with the 2-5 Tarboro Goobers and 3-4 Dun Laoghaire
Messiahs.  The Zzyzx Loadies and Winnetka Baha'i each allowed 57 runs, and the 
Loadies had the porest run margin with -22.

HITTER OF THE WEEK: 
Newfoundland's KEN GRIFFEY went apeshit, creating 19.6 runs in 35 plate 
appearances with a batting line of 548/571/1065 with 4 homeruns and 16 RBIs.
Runners up were Evanston's Bernard Gilkey (12.3), Cincinnati's Ken Caminiti
(11.8), Felldownhead's Alex Rodriguez (11.6), Chinatrust's Jim Eisenreich (11.4),
Cincinnati's Rafael Palmeiro (10.4), Winnetka's Frank Thomas (10.4) and Okoboji's
Mike Piazza (10.2).

BAD HITTER OF THE WEEK:
Rocky Mountain's GARY DISARCINA managed an OPS of 195 in 21 palte appearances.

STARTER OF THE WEEK:
Barcelona's CURT SCHILLING pitched 17 innings, allowing only 2 runs on 7 hits
and 2 walks while striking out 20.

RELIEVER OF THE WEEK:
Butt Mountain's JERRY DIPOTO pitched 4.1 scoreless innings, allowing one hit
and striking out three.

BAD PITCHER OF THE WEEK:
Zzyzx's ALEX FERNANDEZ is clearly broken.  He made two starts, lasting 2.1 innings
in total.  He gave up 14 runs, on 16 hits (but no walks!) and 3 homeruns.
