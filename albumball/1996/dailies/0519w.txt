WEEKLY AWARDS FOR WEEK ENDING MAY 19TH

GOOD TEAM OF THE WEEK:
The OKOBOJI FAMERS AND Cincinnati Reds each went 6-1.  Okoboji lead the league
in run margin, scoring 50 runs and allowing 24 for a margin of +26.
Cincinnati led the league in runs scored with 60; The 5-2 Butt Mountain Beavis
allowed the fewest runs with 10!

BAD TEAM OF THE WEEK:
The GARMISCH-PARTENKIRCHEN CHIM-CHIM SHEBAZZLE! were 1-6 but were only outscored
by 11 runs.  The Zzyzx Loades scored only 18 runs and the Tewksbury Bobs allowed
57 and had a -22 run margin.

HITTER OF THE WEEK: 
Baraboo's JEFF BAGWELL created 18.6 runs in 32 plate appearances with a batting
line of 593/656/1000.  It was a strong week for a lot of hitters: Cincinnati's
Eric Davis (17.6), Barcelona's Brady Anderson (16.0), Newfoundland's Ken Griffey
and Tarboro's Moises Alou (15.0).  Rounding out the top 9 were Alex Rodriguez,
Glenallen Hill, Frank Thomas and Geronimo Berroa, each with more than 10 RC.

BAD HITTER OF THE WEEK:
Dun Laoghaire's JOHN VANDER WAL managed a .268 OPS in 27 plate appearances.

STARTER OF THE WEEK:
Butt Mountain's OMAR DAAL pitched 11.1 innings, allowing one run on five hits
plus six walks, striking out 16.

RELIEVER OF THE WEEK:
Evanston's Tim Crabtree threw 7.0 scoreless innings allowing one hit.

BAD PITCHER OF THE WEEK:
Zzyzx's ALEX FERNANDEZ is broken, winning this award for the 2nd straight week.
He made two starts, throwing 9.0 innings, in which he gave up 5 homeruns, 18 
total hits, 4 walks and 13 runs.
