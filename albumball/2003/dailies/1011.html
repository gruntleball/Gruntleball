Down Goes Zimmer!

A bench-clearing brawl between the Barcelona Brewers and the Felldownhead Firsts interrupts the third game of the playoffs in Barcelona. During the fight, 73-year-old Firsts bench coach Don Zimmer charged out of the dugout and tried to tackle Brewers pitcher Pedro Martinez, but Martinez dodged the older man’s blows and threw him to the ground. The next day, Martinez tried to apologize to the injured Zimmer, but the coach demurred. "I was the guy who charged him and threw the punch," he wrote in his memoirs. "To the people who said Pedro beat up an old man I said, ‘No, an old man was dumb enough to try and beat up on Pedro.’"
