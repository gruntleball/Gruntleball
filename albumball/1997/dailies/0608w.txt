BRIGHTEN THE CORNERS LEAGUE

WEEKLY AWARDS FOR WEEK ENDING JUNE 8TH

GOOD TEAM OF THE WEEK:
The TARBORO GOOBERS (Will Lingo) and the TEWKSBURY BOBS (James Bailey) both
were 6-0 for the week.  Tarboro had the best run margin in the league at +24,
thanks to allowing a TOTAL of five runs for the week!  The 5-1 Chinatrust 
Whales (Rob Miller) led the league in scoring with 42 runs.

BAD TEAM OF THE WEEK:
The EVANSTON ENDORPHINS (Thom Henninger) and the FERDINAND MAGELLANS (Mat
Olkin) were both winless with six defeats.  Ferdinand had the poorer run margin
of -14 runs.  The 2-4 Newfoundland Perfectos (John Sasman) scored only 14 runs 
to trail the league, while the 3-3 Felldownhead Firsts (Doug English) allowed 
41 runs to be the most generous team.  Felldownhead and the 1-4 Rocky Mountain 
Oysters (Mark Haverty) tied for the worst run margin at -15.


HITTER OF THE WEEK: 
Zzyzx's SHAWN GREEN led the league with 11.4 runs created.  Fallkirk's Mark
McGwire was the only other player to break ten with 10.4 RC.

BAD HITTER OF THE WEEK:
Newfoundland's FREDDY GARCIA put up a .071 OPS in 14 plate appearances.  
Felldownhead's Alex Rodriguez only managed a .170 OPS and on top of that, he's 
simply not clutch and therefore should be traded to the Whales where such 
unclutchness is tolerated, and dare I say welcomed.

STARTER OF THE WEEK:
Tarboro's ESTEBAN LOAIZA came off the D/L with a vengeance, pitching 14.1
scoreless innings, yielding but six hits and four walks while striking out eight.

RELIEVER OF THE WEEK:
Barcelona's SHAYNE BENNETT threw 4.0 scoreless innings, allowing just one hit.

BAD PITCHER OF THE WEEK:
Garmisch-Partenkirchen's STEVE KARSAY allowed 10 runs on 16 hits plus 5 walks
in only 7.0 innings.
