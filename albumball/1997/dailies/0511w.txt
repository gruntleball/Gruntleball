WEEKLY AWARDS FOR WEEK ENDING MAY 11TH

GOOD TEAM OF THE WEEK:
The FELLDOWNHEAD FIRSTS went 6-0 and led the league in runs scored (40),
fewest runs allowed (18), and logically margin (+22).

BAD TEAM OF THE WEEK:
Both the Newfoundland Perfectos and the Rocky Mountain Oysters went 1-5.  The
Oysters (23-39) were 1 run worse in margin than the Perfectos (26-41).  The
3-3 Tarboro Goobers and the 3-3 Okoboji Farmers only managed to score 20 runs.
The Cincinnati Reds allowed 50 runs, but went 4-2 despite their -10 run margin.


HITTER OF THE WEEK: 
Cincinnati's RAFAEL PALMEIRO created 11.7 runs in 23 plate appearances, and 
Barcelona's Matt Mieske created 10.1 runs in only 12 plate appearances.

BAD HITTER OF THE WEEK:
Okoboji's TONY BATISTA created zero runs and had a .000 OPS in 13 plate 
appearances.

STARTER OF THE WEEK:
Dun Laoghaire's KEVIN MILLWOOD threw a complete game four-hitter, walking two
and striking out FIFTEEN!  Nearly as effective was Ferlin's Kevin Brown who
allowed three hits and four walks, striking out 11.

RELIEVER OF THE WEEK:
Felldownhead's ARTHUR RHODES allowed one hit in 5.1 innings, striking out four.

BAD PITCHER OF THE WEEK:
San Carlos's AARON SELE gave up three homeruns in 2.0 innings, along with a total
of 9 runs on 8 hits and 2 walks.
