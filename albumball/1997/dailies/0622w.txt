BRIGHTEN THE CORNERS LEAGUE

WEEKLY AWARDS FOR WEEK ENDING JUNE 22ND

GOOD TEAM OF THE WEEK:
Three teams were 5-1 for the week; the Baraboo Goitermen (Mike Canter), 
Century Atoms (Randy Stearns) and Felldownhead Firsts (Doug English).  
Of those three, the BARABOO GOITERMEN had the best run margin at +18.  
The team with the best margin for the week was the Tewksbury Bobs at +27 
(James Bailey) who also led the league in scoring (50).  The leading team in 
run prevention was the Century Atoms.


BAD TEAM OF THE WEEK:
The SAN CARLOS QUENTINS (John Manuel) went 0-6 during the week, scoring a league
low 17 runs and allowing 42 for a margin of -25, also worst in the league.  The 
1-5 Zzyzx Loadies (Matt Poole) allowed the most runs with 43.


HITTER OF THE WEEK: 
Tarboro's MOISES ALOU created 17.5 runs in 27 plate appearances with a batting
line of 625/667/1125.  Baraboo's Kenny Lofton (14.4), Ferdinand's Dale Sveum
(10.2) and Rocky Mountain's Eric Karros (10.1) also had big weeks.

BAD HITTER OF THE WEEK:
Dun Laoghaire's RON GANT and San Carlos's MIKE LIEBERTHAL both had rough weeks,
each putting up an OPS of .150.  At least Gant had a couple of RBIS, so I'll
give the nod to Lieberthal.

STARTER OF THE WEEK:
Baraboo's BUTCH HENRY's complete game four-hitter tops the week.  He walked one
and struck out eight.

RELIEVER OF THE WEEK:
Cincinnati's MARIANO RIVERA struck out six in 3.2 innings while allowing only one
hit.

BAD PITCHER OF THE WEEK:
Chinatrust's ALEX FERNANDEZ was really horrible.  He did record one out, but gave
up seven runs on five hits and two walks.
