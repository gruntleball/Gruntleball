BRIGHTEN THE CORNERS LEAGUE

WEEKLY AWARDS FOR WEEK ENDING MAY 25TH

GOOD TEAM OF THE WEEK:
The SAN CARLOS QUENTINS went 6-1 and did that with a modest +5 run margin.
The 5-2 Century Atoms led the league in scoring with 51 runs and the largest
margin at +20, while the 4-3 Chinatrust Whales led the league in run prevention 
with 16.

BAD TEAM OF THE WEEK:
The NEWFOUNDLAND PERFECTOS went 0-7, (17 straight losses and counting) with a
run margin of -16.  The 1-6 Garmisch-Partenkirchen Chim-Chim Shebazzle scored a 
mere 18 runs to trail the league.  The 2-5 Okoboji Cropdusters allowed 58 runs 
to trail the league in run prevention, and also had the poorest margin at -22.


HITTER OF THE WEEK: 
Baraboo's JEFF BAGWELL led the league with 13.2 RC for the week.  Runners up
included Fallkirk's Juan Gonzalez (12.1), Winnetka's Larry Walker (11.9),
Felldownhead's Jeff Reed (11.2) and Ferlin's Jim Thome (10.6).

BAD HITTER OF THE WEEK:
Barcelona's MATT MIESKE started five games and put up a .059 OPS in 17 plate
appearances.

STARTER OF THE WEEK:
Baraboo's KEVIN TAPANI pitched a complete game 3-hitter, walking nobody and
striking out 10. 

RELIEVER OF THE WEEK:
Tewksbury's DARREN DREIFORT pitched seven innings over six games.  He allowed
no hits and one walk, and struck out nine.

BAD PITCHER OF THE WEEK:
Cincinnati's JOEY HAMILTON got lit up twice, giving up 17 runs on 15 hits, 
including 4 homeruns, plus 9 walks, in only 6.2 innings.
