BRIGHTEN THE CORNERS LEAGUE

WEEKLY AWARDS FOR WEEK ENDING APRIL 20TH

GOOD TEAM OF THE WEEK:
Three teams were 5-1 this week: The Chinatrust Whales, Barcelona Brewers and 
Century Atoms.  The Brewers were the most dominant of the three, scoring 53 
runs, which lead the league, and giving up 33 (which did not).  The Tewksbury
Bobs (3-3), were the stingiest, allowing a mere 15 runs.  Luck was not on their
side this week.

BAD TEAM OF THE WEEK:
The Dun Laoghaire Messiahs went 1-5 and also had the poorest run margin at -25.
They only socred 15 runs (lowest in the league) while allowing 40.  The Winnetka
Baha'i allowed 51 runs for the week, despite the best efforts of Allen Watson.

HITTER OF THE WEEK: 
Barcelona's Brady Anderson created 13.6 runs for the week with a batting line 
of 500/548/857.  Rounding out the field were Barcelona's Javy Lopez (11.0), 
Fallkirk's Matt Williams (10.6) and Baraboo's Jeff Kent (10.0).

BAD HITTER OF THE WEEK:
Tarboro's Travis Fryman made four starts and piled up a .111 OPS in 18 plate
appearances.

STARTER OF THE WEEK:
Zzyzx's Jason Dickson made a couple of starts, including a complete game shutout,
and for the week, allowed 2 earned runs on 9 hits and a walk in 15.0 innings. 
The best single game performance was Dun Laoghaire's David Cone's eight inning 
shutout where he allowed 2 hits and 3 walks while striking out 10.

RELIEVER OF THE WEEK:
Barcelona's Jerry DiPoto appeared in four games, tossing 4.2 scoreless innings
in which he gave up a total of two hits and no walks while striking out four.

BAD PITCHER OF THE WEEK:
San Carlos's Tim Worrell pitched 1.1 innings over two games, but he was busy!
He gave up 11 runs on 8 hits plus 3 walks.
