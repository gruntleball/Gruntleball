BRIGHTEN THE CORNERS LEAGUE

WEEKLY AWARDS FOR WEEK ENDING JUNE 1ST

GOOD TEAM OF THE WEEK:
The GOITERMEN OF BARABOO were 6-0 for the week, leading the league in runs 
scored (57), fewest runs allowed (16) and obviously run margin (+41 for the
math impaired).

BAD TEAM OF THE WEEK:
The OYSTERS OF ROCKY MOUNTAIN were 0-6.  They tied for last in runs scored
(18) and allowed the most runs (59) and of course had the worst run margin
at -41.  The Zzyzx Loadies also had trouble hitting, scoring 18 runs, and
Okoboji was close behind with 19 runs scored.


HITTER OF THE WEEK: 
Baraboo's JON NUNNALLY led the league with 12.1 RC for the week.  Runners up
included Winnetka's Vinny Castilla (10.8) and Baraboo's Tony Gwynn (10.3)

BAD HITTER OF THE WEEK:
Ferdinand's STEVE FINLEY had an ugly week, going 0 for 21 with a walk.  Time
to break out the chicken bone cross.

STARTER OF THE WEEK:
Baraboo's JUSTIN THOMPSON threw 15 innings, allowing two runs, one earned, on 
four hits plus six walks while striking out 16.  Ferlin's Steve Trachsel had 
the best single game of the week, allowing two hits and a walk over nine innings.

RELIEVER OF THE WEEK:
Ferdinand's JIM MECIR allowed one walk in 4.1 innings.

BAD PITCHER OF THE WEEK:
Chinatrust's JOHN FRASCATORE made three appearances, and allowed 7 runs on 5
hits and 4 walks in only 2.0 innings.
