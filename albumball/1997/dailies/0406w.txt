BRIGHTEN THE CORNERS LEAGUE

WEEKLY AWARDS FOR WEEK ENDING APRIL 6TH

GOOD TEAM OF THE WEEK:
The Evanston Endorphins were a perfect 6-0 and are dominating with a +33 run
margin.  The Tewksbury Bobs have allowed only 7 runs in 5 games.

BAD TEAM OF THE WEEK:
The Newfoundland Perfectos were also perfect at 0-6 and not surprisingly had
the worst run margin of -24.  The Zzyzx Loadies allowed 44 runs for the week,
and the Fallkirk Frightened Rabbits have shown great futility in scoring runs
with only 11.

HITTER OF THE WEEK: 
Evanston's Jason Kendall created 14.5 runs in 29 plate appearances, and nobody
else was even close.  Rounding out the top 5 were Ferlin's Edgardo Alfonzo (9.7),
Century's Manny Ramirez (9.4), Zzyzx's Sammy Sosa (9.2) and Evanston's Jeff
Cirillo (9.0).

BAD HITTER OF THE WEEK:
Fallkirk's Matt Williams had a rough week, going 1 for 20 with a walk.

STARTER OF THE WEEK:
Evanston's Dustin Hermanson pitched a complete-game one-hitter, walking three
and striking out nine.

RELIEVER OF THE WEEK:
Felldownhead's Arthur Rhodes and Tewksbury's John Wetteland were both perfect, 
over four innings, but I'll give the nod to Wetteland who appeared in more games.

BAD PITCHER OF THE WEEK:
I typically like to give this to a pitcher who was bad more than one outing, but
sometimes an outing is so bad it has to be called out.  Chinatrust's Shigetoshi
Hasegawa pitched 0.2 innings, and allowed 8 runs on six hits (two of them homeruns) 
plus two walks for an ERA of 81.00.
