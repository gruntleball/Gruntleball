SRL Daily - September 24, 1988

Whales mathematically alive, 3 games behind McDougalls with 3
to play.  Gigantic, (our division) has 3 off days.  On 9/28,
Marshall goes to Tewksbury for their 3 final games, and the
Whales go to Reykjavik for their final three.  If we miraculously
end in a tie, we play a 1-game tiebreaker with our existing
regular-season rosters.

Honestly, Ferlin has as good a shot at Marshall as I do, given
that he has two more games.

When the league was formed in 1999, Kevin, somewhat prophetically, 
included a tagline, "Rest assured, it will be dramatic."  Quite 
appropriate.
