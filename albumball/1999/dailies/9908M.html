Hitters of the Month

Felldownhead's Jeff Reboulet had the poorest month among regulars, batting 059/182/071 in 99 plate appearances. Wow, that's shitty.

Pitchers of the Month

Baraboo's Eric Milton saved his team 18.5 runs compared to the average pitcher. He pitched 45.2 innings with a 1.97 ERA. Runners-up were Barcelona's Pedro Martinez who saved his team 18.3 runs for the month, tossing 45.0 innings with a 1.40 ERA. Rounding out the field were Ferlin's Kevin Brown saving 14.5 runs above average, Felldownhead's Jamie Moyer (11.6), Barcelona's Mike Hampton (10.9) and Garmisch-Partenkirchen's Javier Vazquez with 10.4 runs prevented.

Reliever of the month goes to Garmisch-Partenkirchen's Doug Brocail who saved 8.7 runs above average and did not allow a run in 20 innings over 15 games. Tewksbury's Keith Foulke saved 7.2 runs in 22.2 innings. French Lick's Alan Levine who saved 6.2 runs last month saved another 6.3 runs this month!

The worst pitcher of the month was Newport's Rick Helling who cost his team 12.8 runs with 28.1 innings of 9.85 ERA ball.
