Pitchers of the Month

McCuller's Kevin Millwood saved his team 15.0 runs compared to the average pitcher. He pitched 39.1 innings with a 2.06 ERA. Right behind him was Barcelona's Pedro Martinez who saved his team 14.3 runs for the month, tossing 41 innings with a 1.98 ERA. Rounding out the top five were Baraboo's Eric Milton (13.6), Dun Laoghaire's Ismael Valdes (11.8) and Evanston's Omar Daal (11.5). Reliever of the month was Tewksbury's Keith Foulke, who saved 10.1 runs above average AS A RELIEVER! with 21.1 innings and a 1.27 ERA.

The worst pitchers of the month were Tarboro's Chris Holt who cost his team 16.2 runs with 19.1 innings of 10.24 ERA ball. Also pretty awful were Newport's Armando Reynoso (-15.5) and French Lick's Ryan Franklin (-14.1).
