Pitching Leaders

Chinatrust's Randy Johnson pitched two complete games, one of them a perfect game and saved 9.3 runs above average. In those 18 innings, he gave up two runs on three hits and three walks, striking out 28. First runner-up was Barcelona's Mike Hampton who seems to like the change of scenery. He pitched 16 innings and gave up one run on 6 hits and 6 walks, striking out 17, saving his team 7.5 runs above average. Rounding out the field were Baraboo's Tim Hudson who saved 4.5 runs above average and Cincinnati's Hideo Nomo with 4.3 RSAA.

Reliever of the week goes to French Lick's Alan Levine who saved 3.9 runs above average over 8.1 innings.

Bad pitcher of the week was Felldownhead's Kyle Farnsworth who cost his teams 9.6 runs compared to the average pitcher. Also getting lit up were Okoboji's Al Leiter, Tarboro's Chris Holt and Fallkirk's Dan Smith.
