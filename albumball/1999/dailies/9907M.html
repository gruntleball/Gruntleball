Hitters of the Month

Baraboo's Jeff Bagwell created 45.3 runs with a 1.363 OPS and nine homeruns. He had 125 plate appearances with a batting line of 432/552/811. Runners up were Cincinnati's Rafael Palmeiro (406/479/822) and 41.3 RC, Baraboo's Ray Lankford (462/485/828/37.0), Dún Laoghaire's Larry Walker (356/458/862/36.3) and Ferdinand's John Jaha (371/451/743/35.9).

Dún Laoghaire's Mike Bordick had the poorest month among regulars, batting 155/247/202 in 100 plate appearances.

Pitchers of the Month

Chinatrust's Randy Johnson saved his team 20.4 runs compared to the average pitcher. He pitched 47.1 innings with a 2.09 ERA, including a perfect game. Runners-up were Barcelona's John Smoltz who saved his team 13.1 runs for the month, tossing 38.1 innings with a 1.88 ERA. Right behind him was teammate Pedro Martinez saving 12.6 runs above average, Evanston's Terry Mulholland (11.8) and McCuller's Kevin Millwood (10.9).

Reliever of the month were French Lick's Alan Levine and Baraboo's Trevor Hoffman who each saved 6.4 runs above average. Right behind them were Baraboo's John Wasdin who saved 6.2 runs and Ferlin's Scott Sullivan who saved 6.1 runs.

The worst pitchers of the month was Tarboro's Chris Holt who cost his team 21`.3 runs with 21.2 innings of 15.20 ERA ball.
