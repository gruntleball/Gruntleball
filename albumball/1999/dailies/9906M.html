Pitchers of the Month

Newport's Roy Halladay saved his team 11.8 runs compared to the average pitcher. He pitched 37.2 innings with a 2.87 ERA. Runners-up were McCullers's Kevin Millwood who saved his team 11.2 runs for the month, tossing 35 innings with a 2.57 ERA. After that it drops off a bit, with Ferdinand's Kris Benson saving 8.9 runs above average, Baraboo's Jose Rosado (8.6) and Ferlin's Gil Heredia (8.2).

Reliever of the month was Felldownhead's Sean Lowe, who saved 7.3 runs above average with 20.1 innings and a 1.77 ERA, followed by Garmisch-Partenkirchen's Paul Abbott who saved 7.2 runs for the Shebazzle!

The worst pitchers of the month were French Lick's LaTroy Hawkins who cost his team 23.1 runs with 20 innings of 15.30 ERA ball. Also awful was Fallkirk's Andy Pettitte who cost his team 21.7 runs.
