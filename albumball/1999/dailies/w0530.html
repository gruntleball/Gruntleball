Pitching Leaders

Evanston's David Wells made two starts, pitching 16 innings and saved 5.8 runs above average. He gave up three runs on nine hits and nine walks, striking out eight. Runners up were Barcelona's John Smoltz with 4.2 RSAA, and Ferlin's Kevin Brown who saved 3.9 runs above average.

Reliever of the week goes to Chinatrust's Matt Perisho who saved 2.7 runs above average over 5.0 innings, edging out Newfoundland's Ugueth Urbina who saved 2.6 runs in 5.2 innings.

Bad pitcher of the week was Newfoundland's Chuck Finley who cost his teams 11.2 runs compared to the average pitcher, by virtue of allowing 11 runs in 3.0 innings.
