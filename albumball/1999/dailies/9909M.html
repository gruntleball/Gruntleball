Hitters of the Month

Newport's Matt Stairs created 41.5 runs with a 1.237 OPS and 16 homeruns. He had 139 plate appearances with a batting line of 353/439/798. Runners up were Tewksbury's Carlos Delgado (344/443/854) and 36.3 RC, Newports's Shawn Green (355/434/738/35.6), Ferlin's Edgardo Alfonzo (363/444/613/35.2) and Rocky Mountain's Preston Wilson (342/397/761/33.6).

Fallkirks's Eli Marrero had the poorest month among regulars, batting 106/143/167 in 72 plate appearances.

Pitchers of the Month

Barcelona's Pedro Martinez saved his team 16.0 runs compared to the average pitcher. He pitched 45.2 innings with a 2.17 ERA. Runners-up were Cincinnati's Orel Hershiser who saved his team 13.4 runs for the month, tossing 43.2 innings with a 2.89 ERA. Rounding out the field was Ferlin's Gil Heredia saving 12.9 runs above average.

Reliever of the month goes to Ferlins's Scott Sullivan who saved 9.7 runs above average and allowed 2 runs in 22.2 innings over 12 games. McCullers's Jeff Zimmerman saved 8.3 runs in 18.1 innings.

The worst pitcher of the month was Okoboji's Dennis Springer who cost his team 16.4 runs with 32.2 innings of 9.64 ERA ball.
