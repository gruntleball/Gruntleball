Good Team of the Week:
Five teams went 5-1 for the week:  Goitermen, Endorphins, 101ers, 
Shebazzle! and McDougalls.  Baraboo had the greatest run margin at
+12 so they get the nod.

Bad Team of the Week:
Both the Magellans and the Whales were 0-6, but the Magellans were
outscored by 24 runs, compared to the Whales' 14 run difference.

Hitter of the Week:
Will Clark wins for the second straight week!  He created 15.5 runs 
while compiling a 1.586 OPS for the week.  He went 16 for 29 with 2
doubles and 4 homeruns.  Jeff Blauser was runner-up.

Bad Hitter of the Week:
A Nederland sweep as Gary Redus managed to have negative runs created
for the week, going 1 for 8, but hitting into 2 doubles plays.

Pitcher of the Week:
Dead Guy starter John Farrell pitched 7.1 innings, allowing only 3
hits and no runs.  

For the 2nd week, Doyle Alexander leads the relievers.  He pitched 
6.1 scoreless innings over 5 games.

Bad Pitcher of the Week:
Ferdinand's Bobby Witt allowed 8 runs over just 4.0 innings, so he gets
the nod.
