WOWEE ZOWEE! LEAGUE
Weekly Awards for the Week ending June 25th


GOOD TEAM OF THE WEEK:
The BUTT MOUNTAIN BEAVIS was the only undefeated team this week, going
6-0, scoring 26 runs and allowing only EIGHT!  Dun Laoghaire led the
league in scoring with 58 runs, while the 5-1 Reykjavik 101ers had the 
largest run differential of 41-19.

BAD TEAM OF THE WEEK:
The FELLDOWNHEAD FIRSTS were 1-5, taking the worst title for the second
straight week.  They weren't the only 1-5 team, but they had the poorest
run differential of 15-43.  The GPCC Shebazzle had the weakest offense
(13 runs) the Hong Kong Cavaliers allowed 51 runs.

HITTER OF THE WEEK:
Dun Laoghaire's EDGAR MARTINEZ created 13.5 runs in 30 plate appearances
with a batting line of 435/567/957.  Also contributing strongly to their
respective teams were San Francisco's Tino Martinez (11.9), Hong Kong's
Frank Thomas (11.2) and Dun Laoghaire's Jay Buhner (11.0).

BAD HITTER OF THE WEEK:
Felldownhead's CARLOS HERNANDEZ continues to be truly pathetic.  In 18 plate 
appearances, he put up a batting line of 000/056/000.  He reached base by
virtue of being hit with a pitch.

STARTER OF THE WEEK:
Butt Mountain's Frank Castillo pitched 18 innings, two complete games, one 
shutout, and allowed two runs, one earned.  He surrendered 14 hits and two 
walks while striking out 16.

RELIEVER OF THE WEEK:
Tewksbury's MARK GARDNER was nearly perfect through 4 innings, allowing one
hit and one walk.

BAD PITCHER OF THE WEEK:
Needmore's ANTONIO OSUNA made 4 appearances, pitching 4.1 innings, allowing 
9 runs on 10 hits (including 3 homeruns) plus 2 walks.
