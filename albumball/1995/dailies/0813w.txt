WOWEE ZOWEE! LEAGUE
Weekly Awards for the Week ending August 13th


GOOD TEAM OF THE WEEK:
The FERDINAND MAGELLANS had a 5-1 record for the week and no other teams
matched that.  They scored 30 runs and allowed 16 runs for a +14 margin.
The Barcelona Brewers led the league with 61 runs scored, thanks to a 20-run
outburst against Hong Kong, which also resulted in them having the league-
leading 33 run margin.  The Cincinnati Reds allowed the fewest runs 
with 14.

BAD TEAM OF THE WEEK:
The HONG KONG CAVALIERS were 0-6, with a league worst 69 runs allowed and 
-40 run margin.  The poorest offense was that of the Shebazzle, which scored
only 13 runs. 

HITTER OF THE WEEK:
Barcelona's ROBIN VENTURA led the league with 15.6 runs created in 29 plate
appearances.  He was followed by teammate MAURICE VAUGN with 12.5 RC and
Felldownhead's MARK MCLEMORE with 10.1 RC.

BAD HITTER OF THE WEEK:
Tewksbury's DARRYL HAMILTON put up a .151 OPS in 27 plate appearances.

STARTER OF THE WEEK:
Cincinnati's OREL HERSHISER pitched 15.0 innings, allowing 4 runs on 11 hits 
and a pair of walks.  He allowed no humruns and struck out 8.

RELIEVER OF THE WEEK:
Tewksbury's MARK GARDNER pitched six shutout innings, allowing only one hit
and one walk, striking out four.

BAD PITCHER OF THE WEEK:
Tewksbury's Brian Keyser made two appearances and gave up 7 runs on 6 hits
and a walk in zero point one innings.
