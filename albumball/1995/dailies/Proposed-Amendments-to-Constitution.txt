***CHANGE IN POSITION ELIGIBILITY***

EXISTING RULE:

Hitters may only play positions for which they have defensive ratings, with the following exceptions:
"	CF can play any OF;
"	LF/RF can play the other LF/RF for which they are not rated;
"	LF/RF rated Vg or Ex may play CF;
"	SS can play 2nd base;
"	2nd base can play SS if rated Vg or Ex at 2nd base;
"	Anyone can play 1B.
It is important to note than playing someone at a difficult position for which he has no defensive rating can have dire consequences within the simulation.


PROPOSAL:

DELETE LF/RF RATED Vg OR Ex MAY PLAY CF;
DELETE SS CAN PLAY 2B
DELETE 2ND BASE CAN PLAY SHORTSTOP IF RATED Vg OR Ex AT 2ND BASE


PROPOSED RULE:

Hitters may only play positions for which they have defensive ratings, with the following three exceptions:
* CF can play any OF;
* LF/RF can play the other LF/RF for which they are not rated;
* Anyone can play 1B.
It is important to note than playing someone at a difficult position for which he has no defensive rating can have dire consequences within the simulation.

Existing Rule:
