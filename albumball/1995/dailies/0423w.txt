WOWEE ZOWEE! LEAGUE
Weekly Awards for the Week ending April 23rd


GOOD TEAM OF THE WEEK:
The Barcelona Brewers, Butt Mountain Beavis, Dun Laoghaire Messiahs
and Ferlin Huskies each went 6-1 during the week.  Barcelona had
the highest run margin, scoring 47 and allowing 25.


BAD TEAM OF THE WEEK:
The Tewksbury Bobs went 0-7, scoring 23 and allowing 23 times 2.  The
San Carlos Quentins (1-6) actually were a little worse, scoring 21 and
giving up 46.


HITTER OF THE WEEK:
Cincinnati's Rafael Palmeiro created 12.1 runs in 33 plate appearances
with a 1.392 OPS.  Runners up were Cincinnati's Barry Larkin (11.2), 
Grain Belt's Eric Karros (10.1), Cincinnati's Geronimo Berroa (10.0) and
Ferlin's Ellis Burks (10.0)


BAD HITTER OF THE WEEK:
Felldownhead's Bret Boone had a 0.243 OPS in 30 plate appearances.

	
STARTER OF THE WEEK:
Butt Mountain's Frank Castillo allowed 4 runs on 4 hits and six walks 
in 15.1 innings, striking out 11.


RELIEVER OF THE WEEK:
Barcelona's Dean Hartgreaves allowed only one walk in 6.1 innings,
striking out three.


BAD PITCHER OF THE WEEK:
Grain Belt's Mike Magnante allowed 9 runs in three games.  He threw
3.0 innings, surrendering 9 runs on 12 hits plus six walks.
