WOWEE ZOWEE! LEAGUE
Weekly Awards for the Week ending April 16th.


GOOD TEAM OF THE WEEK:
The Grain Belt Premiums were a perfect 6-0, scoring 45 runs and allowing
28.  The Reyjkavik 101ers were close behind at 6-1, scoring 42 and allowing
29 runs.


BAD TEAM OF THE WEEK:
Two teams--the Garmisch-Partenkirchen Chim-Chim Shebazzle and the
Felldownhead Firsts were winless this week.  Felldownhead had the
"advantage" of an extra scheduled game, however, so they get the nod.


HITTER OF THE WEEK:
Reykjavik's Albert Belle created 16.9 runs in 32 plate appearances.  He
put up a 1.600 OPS (483/531/1069).  Grain Belt's Eric Karros created 
11.9 runs (1.639), Hong Kong's Frank Thomas (11.4/1.374) and Reykjavik's
Sean Berry (1.370/10.4) rounded out the field.


BAD HITTER OF THE WEEK:
Needmore's Pat Borders compiled a 0.133 OPS in 15 plate appearances over
4 starts.

STARTER OF THE WEEK:
Evanston's Scott Sanders pitched 17.0 innings, allowing eight hits and
seven walks, striking out nine.


RELIEVER OF THE WEEK:
Dun Laoghaire's Chad Ogea pitched in 5 games and threw 9.1 innings of 
relief, allowing one run on six hits and a walk, striking out five.


BAD PITCHER OF THE WEEK:
Dun Laoghaire's Jaime Navarro made two starts and lasted only 5.1 innings.
He surrendered 11 runs on 19 hits and three walks.
