WOWEE ZOWEE! LEAGUE
Weekly Awards for the Week ending April 30th


GOOD TEAM OF THE WEEK:
The Reykjavik 101ers were 5-1, scoring 35 runs (2nd in the league) and
giving up 24 (mid-pack).  The 4-2 Chinatrust Whales led the league with
37 runs scored and had the highest run margin (13) and the Butt 
Mountain Beavis, naturally allowed the fewest runs with 11.


BAD TEAM OF THE WEEK:
The Century Atoms and GPCC Shebazzle! were 1-5, though the Shebazzle!
did it with more flair, being outscored 31-15.


HITTER OF THE WEEK:
Butt Mountain's John Valentin created 11.3 runs in 26 plate appearances
while putting up a 1.417 OPS.  Runner up was Barcelona's Kirby Puckett
who created 10.5 RC in 22 plate appearances.


BAD HITTER OF THE WEEK:
San Francisco's Garret Anderson put up a .151 OPS in 27 plate appearances.

	
STARTER OF THE WEEK:
Butt Mountain's Greg Maddux pitched 18 scoresless innings, striking out
15 and allowing 12 hits and one walk.


RELIEVER OF THE WEEK:
Ferdinand's Bruce Ruffin pitched 6.0 scoreless innings, walking 3 and
allowing only one hit while striking out five. 


BAD PITCHER OF THE WEEK:
Evanston's Dustin Hermanson pitched 3.1 innings, allowed 12 runs on 9
hits and 5 walks.  three homeruns
