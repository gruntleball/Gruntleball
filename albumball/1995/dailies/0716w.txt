WOWEE ZOWEE! LEAGUE
Weekly Awards for the Week ending July 16th


GOOD TEAM OF THE WEEK:
The FERLIN HUSKIES went 4-0 scoring 32 and allowing 22 runs.  The league's
leading offense was Beavis with 33 runs scored, and the league's stingiest
pitching and defense was that of the Needmore Perfectos who allowed 12 
runs.  The 3-1 Evanston Endorphins had the best run margin at +18.

BAD TEAM OF THE WEEK:
The GARMISCH-PARTENKIRCHEN CHIM-CHIM SHEBAZZLE! were winless in their
four games, paced by a league-worst 35 runs allowed and -15 run margin.
The Barcelona Brewers have the weakest offense, scoring only 11 runs.	

HITTER OF THE WEEK:
A big week considering only four games were played.  Dun Laoghaire's
MICKEY TETTLETON created 13.2 runs, followed by Butt Mountain's John
Valentin, San Francisco's Carl Everett, Century's Manny Ramirez and
Baraboo's Jeff Bagwell.

BAD HITTER OF THE WEEK:
Felldownhead's MARK MCLEMORE put up a .000 OPS in 15 plate appearances.

STARTER OF THE WEEK:
Evanston's JEFF FASSERO allowed only an unearned run, pitching a complete
game, yielding two hits and a walk while striking out nine.

RELIEVER OF THE WEEK:
San Francisco's RICK AGUILERA allowed only one hit in 4.0 innings,
striking out four.

BAD PITCHER OF THE WEEK:
Grain Belt's PAUL QUANTRILL gave up 6 runs on 7 hits, including two 
homeruns, in only 1.2 innings of work.
