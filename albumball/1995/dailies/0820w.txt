WOWEE ZOWEE! LEAGUE
Weekly Awards for the Week ending August 20th


GOOD TEAM OF THE WEEK:
The FERLIN HUSKIES were a perfect 6-0, scoring 41 runs while allowing 20.
The 5-2 HONG KONG CAVALIERS led the league with 49 runs scored.  The 5-1
TEWKSBURY BOBS allowed a mere 6 runs in 6 games to lead the pitching and
defense category.  The 5-1 REYKJAVIK 101ERS were the most dominant, 
scoring 44 and allowing 11.

BAD TEAM OF THE WEEK:
The GPCC SHEBAZZLE and the EVANSTON ENDORPHINS were each 0-6, but GPCC 
had a ever so slightly worse run margin of -35 runs.  The SAN CARLOS
QUENTINS trailed the league in offense, scoring 11 runs, while the 
EVANSTON ENDORPHINS were quite generous with their 62 runs allowed.

HITTER OF THE WEEK:
Hong Kong's FRANK THOMAS created 17.4 runs in 32 plate appearances.  
Reykjavik's TERRY PENDLETON was next with 13.2 RC.  Rounding out the field
were Ferdinand's TIM NAEHRING (12.2), Tucker's TIM RAINES (12.1) and Grain 
Belt's ERIC KARROS (11.5).  Pendleton just went nuts in his 11 plate 
appearances, with 8 hits, 2 homeruns and 2 walks for a batting line of 
889/909/1556.

BAD HITTER OF THE WEEK:
Butt Mountain's TERRY STEINBACH made 5 starts and put up an 059/059/059 line.

STARTER OF THE WEEK:
Tewksbury's STEVE AVERY made two starts, threw a complete game shutout, and
totaled 16 innings pitched without allowing a run.  He gave up 6 hits and 1
walk while striking out 12.

RELIEVER OF THE WEEK:
Ferlin's BILL RISLEY threw 3.2 innings in 3 games, allowing no runs or hits,
just 1 walk, and struck out 6.

BAD PITCHER OF THE WEEK:
Evanston's TURK WENDELL threw 3.2 innings in 4 games, allowing 10 runs on 11
hits (only 1 homerun) plus 5 walks.  He struck out nobody.
