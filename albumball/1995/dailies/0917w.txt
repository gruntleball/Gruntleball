WOWEE ZOWEE! LEAGUE
Weekly Awards for the Week ending September 17th.


GOOD TEAM OF THE WEEK:
The CENTURY ATOMS were 7-0 and again led the league in both run 
prevention (16) and margin of victory (26).  The GRAIN BELT 
PREMIUMS scored the most runs with 48.  

BAD TEAM OF THE WEEK:
The FELLDOWNHEAD FIRSTS won a game.  They scored 25 runs and gave up
37, which for them isn't that bad.  The Needmore Perfectos scored only
12 runs.  The Cincinnati Reds allowed 54 runs.  The GPCC Shebazzle! 
had the worst run margin at -29.

HITTER OF THE WEEK:
Dun Laoghaire's LOU WHITAKER finished his year with a flourish, creating
16.0 runs in only 22 plate appearances with a batting line of 650/682/1150.
Rounding out the field were Butt Mountain's JEFF CONINE (12.0), Century's
BERNIE WILLIAMS (11.5), Baraboo's KENNY LOFTON (10.7),  Ferlin's CHILI DAVIS
(10.6), GPCC's RAY DURHAM (10.6) and Reykjavik's TERRY PENDLETON (10.0)

BAD HITTER OF THE WEEK:
Needmore's DANNY TARTABULL had 25 plate appearances with a 043/120/043 line.

STARTER OF THE WEEK:
Chinatrust's RANDY JOHNSON threw 11.0 innings (9 as a starter), and allowed
no runs on 3 hits plus 3 walks, while striking out 20.

RELIEVER OF THE WEEK:
Butt Mountains DOUG HENRY (!) pitched four scoreless innings, allowing but a
single walk, striking out two.

BAD PITCHER OF THE WEEK:
Ferdinand's JEFF JUDEN made two starts, pitching 6.1 innings in which he gave up
10 runs on 12 hits (2 HR) and 4 walks.
