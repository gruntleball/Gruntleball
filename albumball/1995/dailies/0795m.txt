Wowee Zowee! League
Monthly Awards for July 1995


Good Team of the Month:
The CINCINNATI REDS went 20-7 during July, scoring 168 runs and 
allowing only 87, for a nearly 2:1 run margin.  Also 20-7 was the
Butt Mountain Beavis, who also nearly doubled their opponents 
153-79.  The CHINATRUST WHALES (16-11) led the league in scoring 
with 189 runs but were also pretty generous, allowing 159.  The 
stingiest team was BEAVIS with their 79 runs allowed. 

Bad Team of the Month:
The FELLDOWNHEAD FIRSTS managed to win 6 games while dropping 21.
"Led" by their league-worst offense that put up 85 runs, they also
allowed the most runs at 185.  Ugh.

Hitter of the Month:
Hong Kong's FRANK THOMAS created 37.5 runs in July and was 5.3 RC
better than Ferlin's CHILI DAVIS with 32.2 RC.  Rounding out the 
field were Ferlin's JIM THOME (32.0), Butt Mountain's JOHN VALENTIN
(30.5), and Century's MANNY RAMIREZ (30.1).

Bad Hitter of the Month:
Felldownhead's MARK MCLEMORE put up a .409 OPS during the month,
starting all 27 games.

Starter of the Month:
Butt Mountain's GREG MADDUX allowed 6 runs for the MONTH.  In 42.2 
innings, he allowed 22 hits and 3 walks, striking out 38 and serving
up only two homeruns.

Reliever of the Month:
Barcelona's DAVE VERES appeared in 16 games, threw 18.0 innings,
allowing 3 runs, zero earned, on 5 hits and 9 walks, striking out 21.

Bad Pitcher of the Month:
The winless PAUL WAGNER of Garmisch-Partenkirchen pitched 25.2 innings,
allowing 38 runs on 48 hits plus eight walks, as well as eight homeruns.
