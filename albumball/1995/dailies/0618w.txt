	WOWEE ZOWEE! LEAGUE
Weekly Awards for the Week ending June 18th


GOOD TEAM OF THE WEEK:
The BUTT MOUNTAIN BEAVIS, San Francisco Arboles and Grain Belt Premiums
all went 6-1 during the week.  Beavis had the league in run prevention
(shocking!) with 18 runs allowed, but San Francisco allowed a mere 19
themselves.  Dun Laoghaire scored 60 runs in 7 games to lead the league.

BAD TEAM OF THE WEEK:
Oddly enough, it wasn't the Whales.  The FELLDOWNHEAD FIRSTS were 1-6
as were the Tucker Tools Police, which prompted a white flag from Josh
Boyd earlier this week.  Felldownhead had the poorest offense, scoring
21 runs.  Needmore had the worst pitching, coughing up 53 runs.


HITTER OF THE WEEK:
Grain Belt's TIM SALMON created 14.0 runs with a batting line of 
481/588/815.  Runners up where Frank Thomas (12.0), Rafael Palmeiro (11.8),
Edgar Martinez (11.5), Tony Phillips (11.2), Tim Raines (10.3) and Jason
Giambi (10.0) in only FOUR plate appearances.

BAD HITTER OF THE WEEK:
Felldownhead's CARLOS HERNANDEZ is truly pathetic.  In 25 plate appearances,
he put up a batting line of 000/040/000 -- a solitary walk.

STARTER OF THE WEEK:
Grain Belt's JASON ISRINGHAUSEN pitched 15.2 innings, allowing three runs on
8 hits plus 5 walks while striking out 13.

RELIEVER OF THE WEEK:
Tewksbury's JOHN WETTELAND was perfect through 4 innings, striking out seven.

BAD PITCHER OF THE WEEK:
Needmore's ARIEL PRIETO coughed up 11 runs in 6.0 innings.
