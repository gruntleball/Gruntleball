WOWEE ZOWEE! LEAGUE
Weekly Awards for the Week ending August 6th


GOOD TEAM OF THE WEEK:
The BUTT MOUNTAIN BEAVIS and SAN CARLOS QUENTINS were 6-1 for the week, 
but Beavis destroyed the competition, scoring a league-high 79 runs and
allowing a mere 15, both league bests by a considerable margin.

BAD TEAM OF THE WEEK:
Three teams were 1-6 this week: The GARMISCH-PARTENKIRCHEN CHIM-CHIM 
SHEBAZZLE!, The TUCKER TOOLS POLICE and the CHINATRUST WHALES.  

HITTER OF THE WEEK:
Butt Mountain's MARK MCGWIRE led the league with 14.0 runs created, followed
by teammate JEFF KING with 13.3 RC.  Rounding out the field were Reykjavik's
SAMMY SOSA (13.0), Cincinnati's RAFAEL PALMEIRO (12.4), Hong Kong's LARRY 
WALKER (11.5), Reykjavik's DANTE BICHETTE (10.5) and ALBERT BELLE (10.4) and
Dun Laoghaire's MICKEY TETTLETON (10.1).

BAD HITTER OF THE WEEK:
Chinatrust's TODD PRATT managed one hit in fifteen plate appearances and a 
0.133 OPS.

STARTER OF THE WEEK:
Butt Mountain's GREG MADDUX pitched 17.0 innings allowing 2 runs (0 earned)
on 9 hits and one walk while striking out 13.

RELIEVER OF THE WEEK:
Dun Laoghaire's "Turnpike" MIKE WILLIAMS pitched 9.0 scoreless innings of
relief allowing 5 hits and a walk, striking out 4.

BAD PITCHER OF THE WEEK:
GPCC's STEVE PARRIS appeared in 4 games pitching 5.0 innings, in which he 
allowed 15 runs on 20 hits, including 1 homerun, plus 4 walks.
