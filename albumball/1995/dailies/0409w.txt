WOWEE ZOWEE! LEAGUE
Weekly Awards for the Week ending April 9th.


GOOD TEAM OF THE WEEK:
The Reykjavik 101ers (KG) were 5-0 during the week, scoring 34 runs and
allowing only 11--a 3:1 ratio.  The surprising San Francisco Arboles
(Kimmey) and the Chinatrust Whales (me!) were each 4-1.   Chinatrust
led the league in scoring, albeit at altitude with 63 runs in five games
and Reykjavik allowed only 11 runs in five games.


BAD TEAM OF THE WEEK:
Three teams were 1-4. The Baraboo Goitermen (Canter), Evanston Endorphins
(Henninger) and Felldownhead Firsts (English).  The Firsts were outscored
by the greatest margin, scoring 13 runs and allowing 35.


HITTER OF THE WEEK:
Evanston's Raul Mondesi created 11.5 runs in 18 plate appearances with
a 1.788 OPS.  Butt Mountain's Mark McGwire also created double digit runs 
(10.8) in 19 plate appearances.  Rounding out the top 5 were Century's
Jose Canseco, Chinatrust's Bobby Bonilla and Grain Belt's Eric Karros.


BAD HITTER OF THE WEEK:
Butt Mountain's Mark Whiten compiled a 0.143 OPS in 14 plate appearances.


STARTER OF THE WEEK:
Barcelona's Curt Schilling and Greg Maddux were for all intents and 
purposes equally dominant, but Schilling has a microscopic edge so he
gets the nod.


RELIEVER OF THE WEEK:
Grain Belt's Julian Tavarez pitched 4.2 shutout innings, allowing one hit
and one walk, striking out four.


BAD PITCHER OF THE WEEK:
San Carlos's Toby Borland made three appearances, pitching 2.2 innings and 
allowing 10 runs on 10 hits, including two homeruns.
