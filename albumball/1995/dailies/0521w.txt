WOWEE ZOWEE! LEAGUE
Weekly Awards for the Week ending May 21st


GOOD TEAM OF THE WEEK:
The Grain Belt Premia and Tucker Tools Police each were 6-1 for the
week.  Tucker, however, was slightly more dominant, outscoring their
opponents by a total of 12 runs compared to 7 for Grain Belt.

BAD TEAM OF THE WEEK:
Three teams went 1-6, the San Carlos Quentins, Evanston Endorphins and
the GPCC Shebazzle!  Evanston sucked the most, being outscored by 19.

HITTER OF THE WEEK:
Andrew, I shit you not.  Garmisch-Partenkirchen's Harold Baines led the
league in RC this week with 13.0.  Harold put up a 1.524 OPS in 28 plate
appearances.  Runners up were Ferdinand's Glenallen Hill, Cincinnati's
Rafael Palmeiro, Tucker's Jay Bell and Needmore's Ken Griffey.

BAD HITTER OF THE WEEK:
Garmisch-Partenkirchen's Jacob Brumfield managed a .182 OPS in 22 plate
appearances.
	
STARTER OF THE WEEK:
Butt Mountain's Pete Schourek allowed three runs total over two starts,
tossing 15.0 innings.  He gave up 8 hits and 3 walks while striking out
16.

RELIEVER OF THE WEEK:
Ferdinand's Ricky Bottalico threw 5.1 shutout innings, allowing 1 hit
and two walks.

BAD PITCHER OF THE WEEK:
Evanston's Roberto Hernandez had a rough week.  He pitched in two games,
lasted 1.2 innings and coughed up 9 runs on 9 hits and two walks.
