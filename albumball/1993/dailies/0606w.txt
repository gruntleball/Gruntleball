FRANK BLACK LEAGUE
Weekly Awards for the Week ending June 6th.


GOOD TEAM OF THE WEEK:
The Chinatrust Whales went 6-0 during the week, scoring 59 runs
and allowing 26 for a +33 margin.  The Grain Belt Premiums were
also 6-0, scoring 37 runs and allowing 21.

THE A-ROD SWEEPSTAKES TEAM OF THE WEEK:
The Felldownhead Firsts, Baraboo Goitermen and Wilken Tools Police
were all 1-5, and both Felldownhead and Wilken were outscored by
18 runs.

HITTER OF THE WEEK:
Chinatrust's Robby Thompson created 12.3 runs in 28 plate appearances
with a batting line of 500/607/909.  Rickey Henderson of GPCC was
runner-up, creating 10.7 runs.

BAD HITTER OF THE WEEK:
Wilken's Steve Buechele had 24 mostly futile plate appearances with 
an average OPS of .214.

STARTER OF THE WEEK:
Ferdinand's Tom Gordon tossed 14 innings and allowed only 2 runs on
6 hits and 5 walks while striking out 13.

RELIEVER OF THE WEEK:
Dun Laoghaire's Albie Lopez pitched 6 shutout innings in 3 games, 
allowing only 1 hit and 1 walk.

BAD PITCHER OF THE WEEK:
Houston's Mark Petkovsek made three appearances totaling 3 innings
where he coughed up 9 runs on 11 hits, including 2 homeruns.
