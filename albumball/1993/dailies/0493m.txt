Good Team of the Month:
The Chinatrust Whales were 19-5 for the month of April, scoring 134
runs and allowing 81.  Their 53-run margin was exceeded by both 
Needmore (18-6, 76 runs) and Butt Mountain (18-7, 62 runs).

Bad Team of the Month:
Four teams tied for the worst record at 7-18.  The Grain Belt Premiums
win the tie-breaker by virtue of their inferior run margin of -65 runs.
They were followed closely by Houston at -62 runs.

Hitter of the Month:
Hong Kong's Frank Thomas created 36.7 runs during April with a 1.196
OPS.  For the month, his line was 413/453/743 with 9 homeruns.  
Needmore's John Olerud (33.3 RC) and Wilken's Paul Molitor (31.8) were
the runners up.

Bad Hitter of the Month:
Dun Laoghaire's Ivan Calderon made 16 starts and managed a .301 OPS.

Starter of the Month:
Needmore's Danny Darwin wins it in a runaway.  Five runs in five starts,
three earned runs, 27 baserunners in 41 innings and a 0.66 ERA.

Reliever of the Month:
Wilken's Tom Henke allowed no runs in 10 games and 12.1 innings pitched.
He allowed only four hits and five walks while striking out 13.

Bad Pitcher of the Month:
Houston's Mike Bielecki allowed 45 baserunners and 25 runs in 20.1 innings.
