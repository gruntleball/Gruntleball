FRANK BLACK LEAGUE
Weekly Awards for the Week ending April 25th.


GOOD TEAM OF THE WEEK:
The Butt Mountain Beavis had a perfect 7-0 week.  They also tied
for the most runs scored with 46 and allowed 2nd fewest with 19, 
for a league-leading run margin of +27.

THE A-ROD SWEEPSTAKES TEAM OF THE WEEK:
The Evanston Endorphins were 0-6.  The worst run margin belonged
to the San Francisco Arboles who scored 14 runs and allowed 41
in six games.

HITTER OF THE WEEK:
Hong Kong's Dean Palmer lead the league with 13.9 runs created.
His batting line was 474/615/1105.  He seems to enjoy altitude.
Wilken's Carlos Baerga was runner up with 12.6 RC.  Other double-
digit honorable mentions: Greg Vaughn, Gregg Jefferies, Todd 
Zeile and Robby Thompson.

BAD HITTER OF THE WEEK:
Evanston's Charlie Hayes managed a .182 OPS in 22 plate appearances.

STARTER OF THE WEEK:
Needmore's Danny Darwin wins for the 2nd time in three weeks, allowing
3 hits and no walks in a complete game, striking out eight.

RELIEVER OF THE WEEK:
Reykjavik's Marvin Freeman made three appearances totaling five innings
and allowed no hits, two walks and struck out six.

BAD PITCHER OF THE WEEK:
Hong Kong's Pete Schourek allowed 10 runs in 5.0 innings, courtesy of 14
hits and two walks.
