FRANK BLACK LEAGUE
Weekly Awards for the Week ending August 15th.


GOOD TEAM OF THE WEEK:
he Century Atoms had the best record (6-1) and best run margin of the
week, scoring 53 runs and allowing a mere 20.  The GPCC Shebazzle!, 
Chinatrust Whales and Tewksbury Bobs were also 6-1.

THE A-ROD SWEEPSTAKES TEAM OF THE WEEK:
Both the Hong Kong Cavaliers and Felldownhead Firsts were 0-7, but 
Felldownhead exhibited greater ineptitude, scoring only 15 runs while
allowing 65.

HITTER OF THE WEEK:
Garmisch-Partenkirchen's Rickey Henderson created 17.3 runs in 34 plate
appearances with a batting line of 440/588/1000.  Off in the distance 
were Butt Mountain's Juan Gonzalez (12.0), Evanston's Mark Grace (11.9)
and Ferdinand's Craig Biggio (11.1).

BAD HITTER OF THE WEEK:
Needmore's Danny Tartabull's 17 plate appearance were exercises in futility
as he amassed an OPS of .118 during the week.

STARTER OF THE WEEK:
Tewksbury's Kevin Gross threw 2 complete game shutouts.  I think that's 
good enough, don't you?  In 18 innings, he allowed 9 hits, 1 walk and
struck out 11.

RELIEVER OF THE WEEK:
Needmore's Jeff Russell pitched 7.1 scoreless innings, yielding a hit 
and 2 walks while striking out 8.

BAD PITCHER OF THE WEEK:
Russ Springer is just amazingly, horrifically bad.  He allowed 12 runs in
6.2 innings on 13 hits and 8 walks.  For the year, his ERA is 15.21 and
batters are hitting 454/521/739 against him.
