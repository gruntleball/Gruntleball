FRANK BLACK LEAGUE
Weekly Awards for the Week ending May 23rd.


GOOD TEAM OF THE WEEK:
The Butt Mountain Beavis and the Ferdinand Magellans both were
6-1 for the week.  Beavis had a +22 run margin to lead the 
league, compared to +21 for Ferdinand.

THE A-ROD SWEEPSTAKES TEAM OF THE WEEK:
The Evanston Endorphins and Felldownhead Firsts were each 1-6.
Evanston, however, was outscored by 19 runs compared to just 
9 runs for FDH.  Grain Belt and Century tied for the worst run
margin at -23 runs.

HITTER OF THE WEEK:
Ferdinand's Barry Bonds and Needmore's John Olerud each created
14.0 RC during the week.  Needmore's Gary Sheffield and Chinatrust's
Paul O'Neill created 12.1 and 11.3 runs, respectively.

BAD HITTER OF THE WEEK:
Houston's Cory Snyder created 0.0 runs with a .125 OPS.

STARTER OF THE WEEK:
Cincinnati's Sid Fernandez pitched 17.0 innings, allowing 5 hits,
3 2alks and struck out 12.  He yielded a total of 3 runs.

RELIEVER OF THE WEEK:
Ferlin's Rod Beck tossed four perfect innings over five games.

BAD PITCHER OF THE WEEK:
Century's Kenny Rogers gave up 12 runs on 13 hits and 2 walks 
in just 4.0 innings.
