FRANK BLACK LEAGUE
Weekly Awards for the Week ending April 18th.


GOOD TEAM OF THE WEEK:
The Evanston Endorphins were 6-1 during Week 2, scoring 37 runs
and allowing 21.  The Needmore Perfectos were 5-1 and had the
largest run margin at +24 runs.

THE A-ROD SWEEPSTAKES TEAM OF THE WEEK:
Houston was a perfect 0-6 and was outscored by a league-worst 27
runs.

HITTER OF THE WEEK:
Hong Kong's Frank Thomas wins for the 2nd straight week, leading
the league with 12.3 RC.  He was followed by Ferdinand's Chris 
Hoiles (11.2), Hong Kong's Lance Johnson (11.0) and Ferdinand's
Barry Bonds (10.8).

BAD HITTER OF THE WEEK:
I'm giving a group award to Dun Laoghaire's Ivan Calderon (.067 
OPS in 15 PA), Kevin Roberson (.077/13) and Bob Melvin (.118/17)

STARTER OF THE WEEK:
Felldownhead's John Burkett's complete game 3-hitter, with one walk
gives him the nod.

RELIEVER OF THE WEEK:
Baraboo's Mark Eichhorn appeared in five games, throwing 5.1 innings
of perfect ball.

BAD PITCHER OF THE WEEK:
Houston's Mark Petkovsek "wins" for the second week in a row, allowing
11 hits and 4 walks on his way to giving up 9 runs in just 3.1 innings.
