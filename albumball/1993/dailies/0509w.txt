FRANK BLACK LEAGUE
Weekly Awards for the Week ending May 9th.


GOOD TEAM OF THE WEEK:
Three teams were 5-1 for the week--the Garmisch-Partenkirchen
Chim-Chim Shebazzle!, the Chinatrust Whales and the Barcelona
Brewers.  Somehow the Brewers managed the feat while outscoring
their opponents by a total of ONE run.  The Whales had the 
best run margin at +33 runs and the Shebazzle! were +27.

THE A-ROD SWEEPSTAKES TEAM OF THE WEEK:
The Felldownhead Firsts were 0-6 for the week and were outscored
by 25 runs.  The San Francisco Arboles had the worst run margin
at -32 runs.

HITTER OF THE WEEK:
Ferdinand's Barry Bonds creatged 17.3 runs with a 464/571/1000
line.  Chinatrust's Kirk Gibson created 13.2 runs in just 15
plate appearances with a 2.250 OPS for the week.  Dean Palmer
of Hong Kong and Rickey Henderson of GPCC were also in double 
digits.

BAD HITTER OF THE WEEK:
Houston's Eric Anthony amassed 2 walks and nothing else in 17
plate appearances.

STARTER OF THE WEEK:
Barcelona's Curt Schilling pitched a complete game one-hitter,
walking four and striking out six.

RELIEVER OF THE WEEK:
Chinatrust's Pedro A. Martinez allowed one hit and nothing else
in 5.0 innings over three appearances.

BAD PITCHER OF THE WEEK:
Hong Kong's Dan Plesac allowed 11 runs on 10 hits, including 3
homeruns plus 5 walks in only 2.1 innings.  Nice work!
