FRANK BLACK LEAGUE
Weekly Awards for the Week ending May 2nd.


GOOD TEAM OF THE WEEK:
The Baraboo Goiterman and Tewksbury Bobs were both 6-1 for the
week.  Tewksbury outscored their opponents by 23 runs compared
to 16 for Baraboo.  The Butt Mountain Beavis were 5-2 and had
the leading run margin 29 runs.

THE A-ROD SWEEPSTAKES TEAM OF THE WEEK:
The Dun Laoghaire Messiahs were 0-7, and also had the worst run 
margin at -28 runs.

HITTER OF THE WEEK:
Needmore's Ken Griffey created 16.9 runs for the week, with a
batting line of 560/607/1080.  Juan Gonzalez of Butt Mountain 
also had a huge week, creating 15.8 runs.  Three other players
were in double digits: Kevin Mitchell, Barry Bonds and Jim
Leyritz.

BAD HITTER OF THE WEEK:
Grain Belt's Ron Gant had 17 futile plate appearances during the
week, going 0 for 16 with 1 walk.

STARTER OF THE WEEK:
Cincinnati's Sid Fernandez pitched 16 innings, allowing 6 hits and
one walk, eeking out the award over the amazing Danny Darwin.

RELIEVER OF THE WEEK:
Evanston's Darren Holmes allowed one hit and no walks in 6.0 innings.

BAD PITCHER OF THE WEEK:
Evanston's Terry Mulholland coughed up 11 runs in ONE inning.
