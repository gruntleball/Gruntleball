CROOKED RAIN, CROOKED RAIN LEAGUE
Weekly Awards for the Week ending April 24th.


GOOD TEAM OF THE WEEK:
The Century Atoms went 6-1 during the week and scored 45 runs
while allowing 30.  The leagues top offense was Baraboo with 51
runs scored and the stingiest pitchers and defense belonged to 
Beavis with 14 runs allowed over 7 games.  Baraboo went 3-4 
despite leading the league with a +23 run margin.

BAD TEAM OF THE WEEK:
Both the Tewksbury Bobs and Dun Laoghaire Messiahs were 1-6,
and the San Carlos Quentins were 1-5.  The Felldownhead Firsts
(2-4) had the worst run margin at -30 runs in six games.

HITTER OF THE WEEK:
Baraboo's Jeff Bagwell created 15.1 runs with a 1.582 OPS for the
week.  Runners up were Century's Ryan Klesko (12.0), Baraboo's
Kenny Lofton (11.9), Cincinnati's Barry Larkin (10.4), Needmore's
Joey Cora (10.3) and Ferdinand's Barry Bonds (10.1).

BAD HITTER OF THE WEEK:
Cincinnati's Ken Caminiti managed a .203 OPS in 25 plate appearances.

STARTER OF THE WEEK:
Greg Maddux didn't allow a run over two starts totaling 16.0 innings,
and surrendered a mere three hits and two walks while striking out 10.

RELIEVER OF THE WEEK:
Century's Jeff Nelson allowed one hit and one walk through 6.1 relief
innings.

BAD PITCHER OF THE WEEK:
San Carlos's Jason Grimsley allowed 12 runs on 11 hits and 7 walks in
only 4.2 innings spanning two starts.
