Hong Kong's Joe Carter and Frank Thomas TIED with 32.9 runs created during
September.  Runners up were Ferdinand's Craig Biggio (32.0), Reykjavik's
Albert Belle (31.0) and Ferlin's Todd Zeile (30.1).

Bad Hitter of the Month:

Starter of the Month:
Tewksbury's Steve Avery made five starts totaling 38.0 innings.  He allowed
8 runs, 7 earned on 18 hits and 12 walks while striking out 37.

Reliever of the Month:
Baraboo's Bobby Ayala allowed no runs in 15.1 innings over 12 games.  He
gave up only 4 hits and 2 walks and struck out 21.

Bad Pitcher of the Month:
Needmore's John Johnstone allowed batters a 1.462 OPS which resulted in 25
