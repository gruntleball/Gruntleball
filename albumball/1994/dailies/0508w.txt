CROOKED RAIN, CROOKED RAIN LEAGUE
Weekly Awards for the Week ending May 8th.


GOOD TEAM OF THE WEEK:
The Baraboo Goitermen were 5-1, as were a number of other teams, and
had the best run margin in the league--having scored 64 runs and allowed
24.  Also 5-1 were Reykjavik 101ers, Felldownhead Firsts (!), Century
Atoms and Tucker Tools Police.

BAD TEAM OF THE WEEK:
The San Francisco Arboles were a perfect 0-7 during the week, scoring 21
runs and allowing 52.  The Ferlin Huskies (1-5) had the league's worst
run margin at -34 runs.

HITTER OF THE WEEK:
Tucker's Shane Mack had an insanely great week, creating 18.9 runs with
a 1.920 OPS.  The runner up was Baraboo's Jeff Bagwell with a mere 12.0
runs created.  Rounding out the top performers was Reykjavik's Dante
Bichette with 10.7 RC.

BAD HITTER OF THE WEEK:
Dun Laoghaire's Eddie Murray created 0.1 runs (0.125 OPS) in 24 plate
appearances.

STARTER OF THE WEEK:
Butt Mountain's John Smoltz made two starts totaling 14.0 innings.  He
allowed 2 runs on 6 hits and 4 walks and struck out 9.

RELIEVER OF THE WEEK:
Evanston's Roberto Hernandez pitched 4.0 scoreless innings of relief, 
giving up one hit and striking out 6.

BAD PITCHER OF THE WEEK:
San Francisco's Doug Jones was a major contributor to their "success" 
this week, allowing 10 runs on 9 hits and 2 walks, over a span of 3
games and 3.1 innings.
