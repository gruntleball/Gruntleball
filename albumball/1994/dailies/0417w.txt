CROOKED RAIN, CROOKED RAIN LEAGUE
Weekly Awards for the Week ending April 17th.


GOOD TEAM OF THE WEEK:
The Butt Mountain Beavis were 6-0 for the week, scoring 40 runs
while allowing only 14.  Hong Kong scored 65 runs in their 7 games
albeit aided by high altitude for some of them.

BAD TEAM OF THE WEEK:
The Chinatrust Whales went 0-6 with a run margin of -24 runs.
The 2-4 Evanston Endorphins scored 13 runs and allowed 38.

HITTER OF THE WEEK:
Hong Kong's Shawon Dunston created 14.2 runs with a 1.379 OPS for
the week (33 plate appearances).  Jim Thome created 14.1 runs in 
only 22 plate appearances with a 1.879 OPS.  Rounding out the field
were Reykjavik's Dante Bichette (12.8 RC) and Hong Kong's Lance
Johnson (12.2 RC)

BAD HITTER OF THE WEEK:
Century's Carlos Garcia had 22 futile plate appearances in six starts,
putting up a .091 OPS.

STARTER OF THE WEEK:
Tucker's Ramon Martinez pitched a complete game 2-hitter, walking 
none and striking out five.

RELIEVER OF THE WEEK:
Barcelona's Danny Cox pitched 3.0 innings over 4 games, allowing only
one walk.

BAD PITCHER OF THE WEEK:
Needmore's Chuck Finley allowed 13 runs on 14 hits and three walks in
only 4.0 innings.
