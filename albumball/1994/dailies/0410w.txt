CROOKED RAIN, CROOKED RAIN LEAGUE
Weekly Awards for the Week ending April 10th.


GOOD TEAM OF THE WEEK:
The Reykjavik 101ers and the Baraboo Goitermen were both 6-1 
for the week.  Reykjavik had a run margin of +34, compared to
+32 for Baraboo.

BAD TEAM OF THE WEEK:
The Chinatrust Whales went 1-6 with a run margin of -24 runs.
The San Francisco Arboles were also 1-6 with a less bad run margin
of -19 runs. 

HITTER OF THE WEEK:
Baraboo's Tim Wallach created 14.2 runs with an OPS of 1.447 for 
the week.  Not far behind were Reykjavik's Mike Piazza (13.9, 1.615),
Ferlin's Chili Davis (13.2, 1.400) and Reykjavik's Albert Belle 
(12.3, 1.408).

BAD HITTER OF THE WEEK:
Barcelona's Robin Ventura (0.0, 0.091 in 22 plate appearances) takes
the "crown" for the opening week.

STARTER OF THE WEEK:
Evanston's Jeff Fassero pitched 17.1 innings with a 1.04 ERA and 
somehow managed to take a loss and not get a win.  He gave up five
hits and six walks while striking out 14. 

RELIEVER OF THE WEEK:
San Carlos's Gil Heredia threw 5.2 innings of relief in three games,
yielding only one hit.

BAD PITCHER OF THE WEEK:
Chinatrust's Rick Sutcliffe didn't retire a batter, but surrendered 
eight runs on four hits and three walks, for an ERA of infinity.
