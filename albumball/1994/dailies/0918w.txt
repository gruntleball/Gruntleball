CROOKED RAIN, CROOKED RAIN LEAGUE
Weekly Awards for the Week ending September 18th

GOOD TEAM OF THE WEEK:
The Tucker Tools Police were a perfect 6-0 for the week, scoring 60
runs and allowing only 16.  Reykjavik was also undefeated at 5-0.

BAD TEAM OF THE WEEK:
The Garmisch-Partenkirchen Chim-Chim Shebazzle! were 0-6, scoring
18 runs and allowing 49.

HITTER OF THE WEEK:
Tucker's Carlos Baerga created 15.7 runs in 28 plate appearances with
a batting line of 607/607/964.  He still loves to take walks.  Runners
up were Ferdinand's Craig Biggio (12.9), Chinatrust's Paul O'Neill (12.3)
Tucker's Kirk Gibson (10.8) and Hong Kong's Frank Thomas (10.6).

BAD HITTER OF THE WEEK:
Tewksbury's Dean Palmer put up an OPS of .095 in 21 plate appearances.

STARTER OF THE WEEK:
Cincinnati's Andy Benes threw a complete game 2-hit shutout, walking
two and striking out ten.  Also throwing complete game shutouts were
Reykjavik's Mike Mussina and Butt Mountain's Greg Maddux.

RELIEVER OF THE WEEK:
Chinatrust's Randy Johnson threw 6.0 shutout innings in relief, allowing
a hit and a walk and striking out 10.

BAD PITCHER OF THE WEEK:
Chinatrust's John Dopson  gave up 10 runs in 5.1 innings on 14 hits 
a walk.  Baraboo's Charles Nagy and Barcelona's Rich Scheid each coughed
up four longballs.
