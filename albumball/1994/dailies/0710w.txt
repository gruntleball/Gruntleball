CROOKED RAIN, CROOKED RAIN LEAGUE
Weekly Awards for the Week ending July 10th


GOOD TEAM OF THE WEEK:
The BARABOO GOITERMEN and REYKJAVIK 101ERS tied for this weeks honor
by virtue of going 5-1 and having identical runs margins of +25.  The
Needmore Perfectos were also 5-1, with a less impressive margin of +15.

BAD TEAM OF THE WEEK:
The GRAIN BELT PREMIA went 0-6, allowing only 22 runs but also scoring
a meager 12 runs.  The Evanston Endorphins had the poorest run margin,
scoring 24 runs and allowing 51.

HITTER OF THE WEEK:
Needmore's GARY SHEFFIELD created 16.6 runs during a week of many strong 
offensive performances.  Runners up were Baraboo's Kenny Lofton (14.9),
Needmore's Ken Griffey (13.1), Ferlin's Marquis Grissom (12.4) and
Ferdinand's Glenallen Hill (12.4 RC in only 14 PA).

BAD HITTER OF THE WEEK:
Grain Belt's DEREK BELL put up a .122 OPS and -0.1 RC in 25 plate 
appearances.

STARTER OF THE WEEK:
Chinatrust's Cal Eldred allowed a total of 2 runs in 2 complete games.  
He allowed 9 hits and 8 walks while striking out eight.

RELIEVER OF THE WEEK:
Century's DENNIS COOK pitched 4.2 scoreless innings, allowing no hits
and 2 walks.

BAD PITCHER OF THE WEEK:
Century's JACK MCDOWELL pitched 8.0 innings and gave up 10 runs on 16 hits,
including 4 homeruns.
