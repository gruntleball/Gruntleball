CROOKED RAIN, CROOKED RAIN LEAGUE
Weekly Awards for the Week ending June 5th


GOOD TEAM OF THE WEEK:
The BARABOO GOITERMEN WERE 7-0 and scored 61 runs while allowing 18, 
equating to the best run margin for the week.  

BAD TEAM OF THE WEEK:
The GRAIN BELT PREMIUMS were 0-6 for the week, scoring 20 runs and 
giving up 43.  The Dun Laoghaire Messiahs had the poorest run margin,
scoring 24 runs while allowing 49.

HITTER OF THE WEEK:
Barcelona's BRADY ANDERSON created 17.5 runs with a 1.727 OPS in 28
plate appearances.  Also having big weeks were Baraboo's Tim Wallach
(15.3), GPCC's Jeromy Burnitz (13.9), Baraboo's Jeff Bagwell (13.9)
and Kevin Mitchell (12.3).

BAD HITTER OF THE WEEK:
Garmisch-Partenkirchen's Dan Wilson put up a 0.077 OPS in 13 plate
appearances over four starts.		

STARTER OF THE WEEK:
Needmore's JIMMY KEY tossed a 2-hit, no walk shutout, striking out
four.

RELIEVER OF THE WEEK:
Century's Dennis Cook threw three shutout innings in four games, 
giving up only a walk.

BAD PITCHER OF THE WEEK:
Dun Laoghaire's Al Leiter pitched 5.2 innings over two starts, allowing
11 runs on 15 hits plus 3 walks.
