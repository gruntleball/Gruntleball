CROOKED RAIN, CROOKED RAIN LEAGUE
Weekly Awards for the Week ending August 14th

GOOD TEAM OF THE WEEK:
Four teams went 6-1 for the week:  Butt Mountain, San Francisco, 
Cincinnati and Dun Laoghaire.  Beavis had the best run margin at
+ 23 runs, scoring 46 and allowing 23, which was the fewest in the
league.  Dun Laoghaire tied for best offense with 53 runs scored.

BAD TEAM OF THE WEEK:
The Barcelona Brewers were 1-6, scoring 37 runs and allowing 55.
Their -18 run margin was eclipsed, however, by Grain Belt with a
margin of -20 runs.  Grain Belt scored the fewest runs (26), and
Barcelona allowed the most (55).	

HITTER OF THE WEEK:
Baraboo's Jeff Bagwell created 13.3 runs to lead the league, followed
by Ferdinand's Barry Bonds with 12.8 and San Carlos's Fred McGriff with
12.4.  Also in double digits were Matt Williams, Karl Rhodes, Shane Mack,
Mike Greenwell and Rusty Greer.

BAD HITTER OF THE WEEK:
Grain Belt's Derek Bell managed a .133 OPS in 15 plate appearances.	

STARTER OF THE WEEK:
Butt Mountain's Roger Clemens allowed 2 runs in 17 innings on 9 hits
and one walk while striking out 17.  Runner up was Greg Maddux who
allowed 2 runs in 18 innings on 10 hits and 2 walks, striking out 8.

RELIEVER OF THE WEEK:
Baraboo's Steve Howe pitched 5.0 scoreless innings, allowing one hit
and striking out three.

BAD PITCHER OF THE WEEK:
Dun Laoghaire's Salomon Torres gave up 17 runs on 19 hits and 5 walks
in 7.1 innings.
