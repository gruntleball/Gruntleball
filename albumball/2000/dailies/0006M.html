Hitters of the Month

New York's Tony Batista created 25.3 runs above average with a .548 wOBA and a fucking 19 homeruns. He had 123 plate appearances with a batting line of 354/407/920! Runners up were Tewksbury teammates Nomar Garciaparra and Carlos Delgado with 22.8 wRAA and wOBAs of 587 and 522, respectively. Rounding out the field was Dinosaur's Frank Thomas with 21.3 wRAA and a .519 wOBA.

Bad hitter of the month was Felldownhead's Doug Mirabelli who cost his team 15.4 runs with a .170 woBA.

Pitchers of the Month

Newport's Kris Benson saved his team 14.3 runs compared to the average pitcher. He pitched 43.1 innings with a wOBA against of .255 -- about half of this at altitude, mind you. Behind him was Baraboo's Scott Schoeneweis who saved his team 12.1 runs for the month with a wOBA against of .263. Rounding out the field was Barcelona's Pedro Martinez and Newfoundland's Robert Person who each saved their team 11.5 runs.

Reliever of the month goes to Dinosaur's LaTroy Hawkins who saved 10.7 runs above average over 18.1 innings with a .176 wOBA. Newfoundland's Robb Nen saved 9.0 runs in 13.0 innings with a small .121 wOBA.

The worst pitcher of the month was Newport's Rick Helling who cost his team 23.2 runs more than an average pitcher, tossing 19.0 innings and coughing up 10 homeruns and with a .566! wOBA. Century's Chris Carpenter served up 12 homeruns in June.
