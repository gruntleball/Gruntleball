Pitchers of the Month

Ferlin's Kevin Brown saved his team 23.0 (holy shit) runs compared to the average pitcher. He pitched 42.0 innings with a wOBA against of only .191. A fair distance back was McCullers's Kerry Wood who saved 16.1 runs in 39.0 innings with a .235 wOBA against. Finally, Evanston's Chuck Finley saved 15.5 runs with a .228 wOBA against in 35.2 innings.

Reliever of the month goes to Baraboo's Trevor Hoffman who saved 14.8 runs (another holy shit!) above average over 21.0 innings with a .143 wOBA. Next best reliever was Okoboji's Jose Jimenez who saved 11.3 runs in 18.0 innings with a .170 wOBA.

The worst pitcher of the month was Chico's Kirk Rueter who cost his team 20.0 runs more than an average pitcher, tossing 31.1 innings and coughing up 10 homeruns and with a .469 wOBA. Garmisch Partenkirchen's Javier Vazquez also served up 10 homeruns in July, and his pitching cost the team 18 runs compared to an average pitcher.
