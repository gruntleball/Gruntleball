Hitters of the Month

Tewksbury's Nomar Garciaparra created 25.7 runs above average with a .545 wOBA and 7 homeruns. He had 126 plate appearances with a batting line of 455/508/800. Runner up was Baraboo's Jeff Kent with a wRAA and wOBA of 23.5 and .543, respectively. Rounding out the top 5 were Newport's Shawn Green (20.8/.516); Evanston's Matt Lawton (20.4/.516); and Ferlin's David Justice (19.1/.494).

Bad hitters of the month were Tarboro's Marlon Anderson (-13.0/.206); Chico's Doug Glanville (-12.1/.199); Baraboo's Rich Aurilia (-12.0/.214); Chico's Mike Matheny (-11.6/.148); and Brooklyn's Wilton Guerrero (-11.2/.198).

Pitchers of the Month

Barcelona's Pedro Martinez saved his team 22.1 runs compared to the average pitcher. He pitched 37.0 innings with a wOBA against of only .170. Runners-up were Newfoundland's Orlando Hernandez who saved his team 17.7 runs for the month, tossing 45.1 innings with a wOBA against of .237, and Evanston's Chuck Finley who saved 1.64 runs with a .233 wOBA against.

Reliever of the month goes to Cincinnati's Ricky Bottalico who saved 9.6 runs above average over 14.1 innings with a .154 wOBA. Felldownhead's Mike Stanton saved 9.3 runs in 14.1 innings with a .153 wOBA.

The worst pitcher of the month was Chico's Mike Lincoln who cost his team 27.5 runs more than an average pitcher, tossing only 14.1 innings and coughing up 15 homeruns! His wOBA against was .638!
