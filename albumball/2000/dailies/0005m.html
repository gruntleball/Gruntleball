Rounding out the top 5 were Newport's Barry Bonds (15.5/.466); GPCC's Bernie Williams (15.1/.452); and Okoboji's Jim Edmonds (15.0/.456).

Pitchers of the Month

Okoboji's Al Leiter saved his team 19.2 runs compared to the average pitcher. He pitched 45.2 innings with a wOBA against of .226. Right behind him was Barcelona's Pedro Martinez who saved his team 19.1 runs for the month, tossing 45.0 innings with a wOBA against of .219. Rounding out the field were Newfoundland's Paul Wilson who saved 15.0 runs with a tiny .150 wOBA against in only 24.0 innings; and New York's Jeff Weaver with 14.2 runs saved.

Reliever of the month goes to New York's Jose Paniagua who saved 12.3 runs above average over 23.2 innings with a .184 wOBA. Chinatrust's Mariano Rivera saved 10.6 runs in 14.1 innings with a minute .108 wOBA.

The worst pitcher of the month was Ferdinand's Jose Lima who cost his team 20.7 runs more than an average pitcher, tossing 37.2 innings and coughing up 10 homeruns and with a .443 wOBA.
