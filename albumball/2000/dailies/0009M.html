Hitters of the Month

Felldownhead's Vladimir Guerrero created 24.6 runs above average with a .547 wOBA and 13 homeruns. He had 120 plate appearances with a batting line of 407/442/876. Right behind him were Newport's Barry Bonds with 22.3 wRAA and a wOBA of .544 and Fallkirk's Jason Giambi with 22.0 wRAA and a .565 wOBA. Rounding out the field was Baraboo's Jeff Bagwell who created 21.5 runs above average with a .535 wOBA in 112 plate appearances.

Bad hitter of the month was Fallkirk's Adam Kennedy (again!) who cost his team 16.8 runs with a .195 woBA.

Pitchers of the Month

McCullers's Kevin Millwood saved his team 18.0 runs compared to the average pitcher. He pitched 34.1 innings with a wOBA against of only .202 and 27 strikeouts. Newport's Denny Neagle who saved 16.7 runs in 49.1 innings with a .255 wOBA against. Following was Tewksbury's Julian Taverez who saved 16.4 runs with a .225 wOBA against in 37.0 innings.

Reliever of the month goes to Tewksbury's Matt Whiteside who saved 11.4 runs above average over 22.1 innings with a .204 wOBA. Next best reliever was Rocky Mountain's Turk Wendell who saved 8.2 runs, also in 21.1 innings with a .241 wOBA.

The worst pitcher of the month was Ferdinand's Scott Downs who cost his team 16.4 runs more than an average pitcher, tossing 22.2 innings and coughing up 7 homeruns and with a .481 wOBA.
