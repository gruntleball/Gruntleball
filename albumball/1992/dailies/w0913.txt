SLANTED & ENCHANTED
Weekly Awards for the week ending September 13th, 1992

Good Team of the Week
The Barcelona Brewers and Dun Laoghaire Messiahs were each 5-1.  Dun
Laoghaire scored 4 more runs, but allowed 6 more, so Barcelona gets 
the nod.

Bad Team of the Week
The Reykjavik 101ers and the Chinatrust Whales were each 1-5.  Reykjavik
had the poorer run margin, so they get the nod.

Hitter of the Week
Tewksbury's Chuck Knoblauch created 9.4 runs in 30 plate appearances
with a 1.179 OPS.  Runners up were Grain Belt's Jeff Blauser 8.5/1.266
(RC/OPS) and Dun Laoghaire's Cal Ripken 7.5/1.200.

Bad Hitter of the Week
Reykjavik's Darrin Jackson created 0.0 runs with a .059 OPS in 17 plate
appearances.

Starter of the Week
Century's Ron Darling pitched 14.0 innings in which he gave up just 7
hits and one walk.

Reliever of the Week
Chinatrust's Bill swift pitched four scoreless innings, allowing just
two walks and nothing else.

Bad pitcher of the Week 
Evanston's Lee Guetterman allowed 8 runs on 10 hits and 2 walks in just
3.0 innings.  He also coughed up two long balls.
