SLANTED & ENCHANTED
Weekly Awards for the week ending August 9th, 1992

Good Team of the Week
The Ferlin Huskies were 5-1 for the week and scored 40 runs while allowing only
12, for a league-leading 28-run margin.  Dun Laoghaire was right on their heels,
however, also 5-1 and outscoring their opponents 36-11.

Bad Team of the Week
The Evanston Endorphins have begun to channel the 101ers with their own double-
digit losing streak.

Hitter of the Week
Butt Mountain's Mark McGwire created 12.2 runs during the week with a 1.536 OPS.
He hit four homeruns and batted 429/536/1000 for the week.  Runner-up was 
Houston's Fred McGriff with 11.8 runs created.

Bad Hitter of the Week
Evanston's Jose Vizcaino created 0.1 runs with a .111 OPS in 18 plate appearances.

Starter of the Week
Grain Belt's Frank Castillo pitched a 2-hit, 1-walk, complete game shutout.

Reliever of the Week
Barcelona's Jeff Montgomery threw six innings and gave up one hit, no walks, and
no runs of course.

Bad pitcher of the Week 
Century's Tim Leary pitched 2.1 innings and gave up 12 runs on 14 hits and a walk.
He also gave up two homeruns.  Ferdinand's Tommy Greene was almost as bad, giving
up 14 runs, but in 4.0 innings.
