SLANTED & ENCHANTED
Weekly Awards for the week ending September 6th, 1992

Good Team of the Week
The Butt Mountain Beavis were 7-0 for the week again, scoring 34 runs and 
allowing a mere 16.  Ferdinand led the league in runs scored with 35, and
the Chinatrust Whales were the stingiest, allowing just 9 runs or 1.8 runs
per game for the week.

Bad Team of the Week
The Baraboo Goitermen and Reykjavik 101ers were both 1-5, but Baraboo had 
a worse run margin, scoring 16 and allowing 30.  Nederland scored the 
fewest runs, six, or 1.2 per game.  Century allowed 32 runs or 5.3 per game.

Hitter of the Week
Butt Mountain's Ryne Sandberg created 12.4 runs, easily leading the field.
Houston's Shane Mack was runner up with 9.0 RC, followed by Grain Belt's
Lee Stevens with 7.6 RC.

Bad Hitter of the Week
For the second straight week, Baraboo's Jody Reed was the worst hitter, 
following his .122 OPS with .095 this week in 21 plate appearances.

Starter of the Week
Dun Laoghaire's Jim Abbott pitched a complete game 2-hitter, walking one
and striking out nine.  Nederland's Frank Tanana tossed a complete game
3-hitter, walking one, striking out four.  And Chinatrust's Anthony Young
also three a three-hit shutout, walking none and striking out six.  

Reliever of the Week
Barcelona's Lee Smith threw 7.2 shutout innings of relief, allowing 5 hits.

Bad pitcher of the Week 
Nederland's Arthur Rhodes was pretty crappy, giving up 6 runs in 2.1 
innings on 7 hits and 2 walks.
