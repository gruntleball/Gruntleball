SLANTED & ENCHANTED
Weekly Awards for the week ending August 23rd, 1992

Good Team of the Week
The Reykjavik 101ers (!) went 4-1, scoring 13 runs but allowing just 8.  The
Barcelona Brewers went 4-2 and had a margin of +9 runs.

Bad Team of the Week
The Baraboo Goitermen were 0-6 and were outscored by 19 runs.

Hitter of the Week
Reykjavik's Albert Belle created 14.2 runs in 22 plate appearances.  For the
week, he batted 579/636/1105 and has pulled his average over .210 for the year.
Barry Bonds was a distant 2nd at 9.5 RC.

Bad Hitter of the Week
Dun Laoghaire's Cal Ripken had a rough week with a .056 OPS in 18 plate
appearances.

Starter of the Week
Houston's Frank Viola pitched 16.0 scoreless innings in two starts, allowing
only 7 hits and 5 walks while striking out 7.  Teammate Scott Erickson also
allowed no runs on just a hit and a walk in 9.0 innings.

Reliever of the Week
Grain Belt's Chuck Crim threw 5.1 innings of relief over two games, allowing
no runs and a single hit while walking no one.

Bad pitcher of the Week 
Baraboo's Storm Davis allowed 7 runs on 10 hits in just 3.0 innings.
