SLANTED & ENCHANTED
Weekly Awards for the week ending September 27th, 1992

Good Team of the Week
The Ferlin Huskies and Houston Streets were each 5-2 for the week.  
Ferlin scored two fewer runs than Houston, but allowed five fewer, so
they get to be called "best."

Bad Team of the Week
The Reykjavik 101ers went 1-5 and were outscored by 22 runs, last in
runs scored, and first in runs allowed

Hitter of the Week
Nederland's Frank Thomas created 12.2 runs in 30 plate appearances with
a 1.373 OPS.  Will Clark of Chinatrust was runner up with 11.3 RC.

Bad Hitter of the Week
Reykjavik's Andujar Cedeno created 0.0 runs with a .167 OPS in 18 plate
appearances.

Starter of the Week
Chinatrust's Anthony Young (of all people) pitched 16.2 scoreless innings
including one complete game shutout.  He walked 5 and surrendered 13 hits.

Reliever of the Week
Ferlin's Rusty Meacham pitched 8.0 scoreless innings in three games, 
giving up a mere four hits and no walks.

Bad pitcher of the Week 
Butt Mountain's Marvin Freeman allowed two hits, four walks but SEVEN runs
in just 0.1 innings.
