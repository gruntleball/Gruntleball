SLANTED & ENCHANTED
Weekly Awards for the Week ending May 17th.

Good Team of the Week
The Butt Mountain Beavis and Chinatrust Whales each were 4-1, but the
Whales were lucky, since they were actually outscored by one run.  
Barcelona was the most dominant with a margin of +22 runs.

Bad Team of the Week
The Century Atoms went 1-5 and were outscored by 6 runs.  The Houston
Streets (2-4) had the poorest run margin per game at -13 runs.

Hitter of the Week
I think this is a record for RC in a week!
Ferdinand's Barry Bonds created 25.6 runs in 27 plate appearances with
a 2.341 OPS.  Yikes.  Barry had 13 hits--4 singles, 4 doubles and 5 
homeruns to go with 7 walks.  The next best batter, Ray Lankford, 
created a mere 12.3 runs with a 1.527 OPS.

Bad Hitter of the Week
Grain Belt's Hal Morris had a 100/100/100 week in 20 plate appearances.

Starter of the Week
Tewksbury's Rick Reed tossed 15 innings, allowing just 6 hits and 3 
walks, while striking out 8.

Reliever of the Week
Evanston's Roberto Hernandez appeared in three games, pitching six innings.
He struck out five while allowing just two hits and one walk.

Bad pitcher of the Week 
Chinatrust's Greg W. Harris lasted just 3.2 innings in his one start.  He
gave up 8 runs on 7 hits and one walk, and served up three homeruns too.
