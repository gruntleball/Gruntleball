SLANTED & ENCHANTED
Weekly Awards for the Week ending April 5th.

Good Team of the Week
The Dun Laoghaire Messiahs (as well as two other teams) were 4-1 in the
season's short opening week.  They outscored their opponents by 14 runs.
The Ferdinand Magellans had the greatest run margin of 17 runs.

Bad Team of the Week
The Baraboo Goitermen went 1-4, as did two other teams, but were out-
scored by 22 runs.

Hitter of the Week
Nederland's Greg Vaughn put up a 1.428 OPS and created 10.0 runs to 
lead the league.  Ozzie Smith and Andy Van Slyke each created 9.0 runs
as runners up.

Bad Hitter of the Week
Baraboo's Jeff Kent was hitless and walkless with 6 strikeouts in 12
plate appearances	

Starter of the Week
Tewksbury's Terry Mulholland pitched a complete game, allowing only
6 baserunners and notching 6 strikeouts.

Reliever of the Week
Evanston's Roberto Hernandez appeared in three games, allowing just two
batters to reach in his five innings of work.

Bad pitcher of the Week 
Baraboo's Jose DeLeon gave up 7 runs on 7 hits and 4 walks in just 3.1
innings.
