SLANTED & ENCHANTED
Weekly Awards for the week ending August 2nd, 1992

Good Team of the Week
The Houston Streets were a perfect 7-0 for the week, scoring 48 runs and 
allowing just 22, the 2nd best margin in the league.  The 6-1 Dun Laoghaire
Messiahs outscored their opponents by 29 runs.

Bad Team of the Week
The Reykjavik 101ers were 0-7 for the second straight week, and were outscored
by 17 runs.  The Baraboo Goitermen were outscored by 24 runs.

Hitter of the Week
Ferdinand's Barry Bonds handily led the league with 19.4 runs created in six games.
Runners up were Dun Laoghaire's Lance Blankenship (16.4 runs) and Barcelona's Chili
Davis (12.2 runs).

Bad Hitter of the Week
Grain Belt's Luis Sojo managed a .091 OPS in 22 plate appearances.

Starter of the Week
Century's Greg Hibbard pitched 10 innings, in one game no less, and allowed just
one unearned run on three hits and no walks, striking out seven.

Reliever of the Week
Effin's Mark Eichhorn three four perfect innings, striking out three.

Bad pitcher of the Week 
Baraboo's Charles Nagy gave up 8 runs in just 3.1 innings.
