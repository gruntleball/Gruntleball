SLANTED & ENCHANTED
Weekly Awards for the week ending July 5th, 1992

Good Team of the Week
The Houston Streets went 6-1 during the week, scoring 40 runs, allowing 22, and
had the largest run margin in the league.  The Tewksbury Bobs were 5-2.

Bad Team of the Week
The Chinatrust Whales were a perfect 0-6 and were outscored by 20 runs.  Apparently
Thomas and Walker were good for team chemistry.

Hitter of the Week
Houston's Fred McGriff created 11.7 runs in 34 plate appearances.  He put up an OPS
of 1.293 with a 414/500/793 line.  Shane Mack created 10.1 runs.

Bad Hitter of the Week
Nederland's Henry Rodriguez put up a .232 OPS in 22 plate appearances.  Chinatrust's
Matt Nokes was pretty bad too.

Starter of the Week
Evanston's Dwight Gooden pitched a complete game shutout, allowing 4 hits and 3 walks
while striking out 5.

Reliever of the Week
Houston's Rich Rodriguez was lights out, throwing 7.1 innings during which he
allows one hit, one walk and struck out two.

Bad pitcher of the Week 
Effin's Dennis Cook gave up 13 runs on 15 hits and 3 walks in 9.2 innings.  Here's 
the best part... SEVEN homeruns in those 9.2 innings.   Ooof.
