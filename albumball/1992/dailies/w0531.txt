SLANTED & ENCHANTED
Weekly Awards for the Week ending May 31st.

Good Team of the Week
The Butt Mountain Beavis went 5-0 and allowed just 10 runs while scoring
27.  The 5-1 Ferlin Huskies were the most dominant, outscoring their
opponents 40 to 20.

Bad Team of the Week
The Reykjavik 101ers at 1-6, and with a run margin of -30 was the most
feeble team this week... by far.

Hitter of the Week
Butt Mountain's Ryne Sandberg created 15.4 runs in 21 plate appearances in
which he put up a line of 611/667/1278.  Shane Mack was a distant runner up 
with 10.4 RC.

Bad Hitter of the Week
Reykjavik's Carney Lansford managed a 214 OPS in 24 plate appearances.

Starter of the Week
Barcelona's Dennis Martinez threw 17 shutout innings.  Works for me.  He
allowed just 8 hits and 2 walks, striking out 10.

Reliever of the Week
Ferlin's Rusty Meacham appeared in just two games, but pitched 5.2 shutout
innings, striking out six.  He allowed no hits and two walks.

Bad pitcher of the Week 
Houston's Ben Rivera made two castastrophic starts in which he lasted all 
of six innings.  He gave up 13 hits and 3 walks and 11 runs.
