SLANTED & ENCHANTED
Weekly Awards for the Week ending June 7th.

Good Team of the Week
The Butt Mountain Beavis went 7-0 and allowed just 8 runs while scoring
34, leading the league in dominance as well.

Bad Team of the Week
The Effin Shebazzle! were 0-7 with a run margin of -16, which barely eeked
past Evanston's margin of -15.

Hitter of the Week
Dun Laoghaire's Dave Winfield created 10.7 runs, putting up a 1.515 OPS in
23 plate appearances.  Nederland's Will Clark was runner-up with 9.8 RC.

Bad Hitter of the Week
Tewksbury's Scott Cooper created 0.3 runs while putting up a .229 OPS in 18
plate appearances.

Starter of the Week
Butt Mountain's Greg Maddux made two starts, pitching 16.0 innings.  He 
allowed just one run on 6 hits and 4 walks, while striking out 15.

Reliever of the Week
Wilken's Steve Howe appeared in 4 games, pitched 5.0 innings and allowed
a solitary walk while striking out three.

Bad pitcher of the Week 
Needmore's Erik Hanson gave up 10 runs in 4.2 innings.  He accomplished 
this by allowing 12 hits and 5 walks.
