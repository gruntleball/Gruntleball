SLANTED & ENCHANTED
Weekly Awards for the Week ending June 14th.

Good Team of the Week
The Ferdinand Magellans were 6-1 for the week, and outscored their 
opponents 45 to 27, giving them the largest run margin at +18 runs.

Bad Team of the Week
The Century Atoms were 0-5 for the week and were outscored by 18 runs,
which wasn't the worst run margin.  That honor belonged to Chinatrust,
which allowed 19 more runs than they scored.

Hitter of the Week
Butt Mountain's Mitch Webster wins this week with just 12 plate appearances.
He made the most of them, creating 12.2 runs with an OPS of 2.303.  Joe
Carter also created 12.2 runs, but did so in 29 plate appearances.  Barry
Bonds (11.3) and Wally Joyner (10.9) rounded out the field.

Bad Hitter of the Week
Baraboo's Rey Sanchez managed a .040 OPS in 25 plate appearances,
which is damned impressive.

Starter of the Week
Barcelona's Dennis Martinez pitched two complete game shutouts, allowing
just 5 hits plus 5 walks while striking out 13.  That's good enough for me.

Reliever of the Week
Cincinnati's Eric Plunk appeared in three games, tossed 4.2 innings, 
allowing just a single walk.

Bad pitcher of the Week 
Chinatrust's Paul Gibson allowed 11 hits and 3 walks, which resulted in
9 runs, in just 4.0 innings.
