SLANTED & ENCHANTED
Weekly Awards for the week ending July 19th, 1992

Good Team of the Week
The Butt Mountain Beavis and Barcelona Brewers were each 4-0.  Butt Mountain was
slightly more dominant, with a 15 run margin to Barcelona's 12.  Butt Mountain 
scored 41 runs to lead the league, and Barcelona allowed 7 runs to lead the
league in run prevention.

Bad Team of the Week
The Reykjavik 101ers were 1-4 for the week, but were only outscored by one run!
The 1-3 Nederland Frozen Dead Guys were outscored 25 to 9 in four games.

Hitter of the Week
Reykjavik's Wally Joyner created 13.0 runs in five games and 24 plate appearances,
putting up a 1.792 OPS.  Paul O'Neill and Dave Martinez were distant runners up
with 9.6 runs created.

Bad Hitter of the Week
Nederland's Ruben Sierra put up a big zero in 12 plate appearances.

Starter of the Week
Barcelona's Curt Schilling allowed just two hits and no walks in 8.0 innings,
while striking out eight.  Pete Harnisch was also strong, allowing two hits and
one walk in 9.0 innings and deserves note as runner-up.

Reliever of the Week
Barcelona's Lee Smith was perfect in his 5.0 innings of relief, and struck out four.

Bad pitcher of the Week 
Effin's Willie Blair had an amazing week.  In two games, he pitched 1.1 innings,
allowing 14 runs on 11 hits and 3 walks, and allowed 3 homeruns.  That's impressive.
