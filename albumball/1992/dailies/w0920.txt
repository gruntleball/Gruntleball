SLANTED & ENCHANTED
Weekly Awards for the week ending September 20th, 1992

Good Team of the Week
The Wilken Tools Police were 5-1 for the week.  Ferdinand, Butt Mountain
and Dun Laoghaire were each 5-2.  Wilken also had the best run margin at
+14 runs.

Bad Team of the Week
The Century Atoms were 1-6 and the Evanston Endorphins were 1-5.  Grain
Belt Premiums had the poorest run margin at -24.

Hitter of the Week
Wilken's Jose Canseco created 13.5 runs and put up a 2.128 OPS for the
week, which is pretty good I think.  He outproduced Roberto Alomar, the
runner-up by with 9.0 RC by 50 percent.

Bad Hitter of the Week
Baraboo's Jody Reed managed a .111 OPS in 19 plate appearances.  He's 
made this list before methinks.

Starter of the Week
Chinatrust's Bill Swift made one start and one relief appearance, 
pitching 10 innings and allowing only 3 hits and 2 walks while striking
out ten.

Reliever of the Week
Wilken's Chris Nabholz pitched 4.0 innings of relief and surrendered only 
a walk.

Bad pitcher of the Week 
Chinatrust's Dave Righetti made two starts totaling 8.2 innings and gave
up 10 hits and 6 walks which resulted in 10 runs.
