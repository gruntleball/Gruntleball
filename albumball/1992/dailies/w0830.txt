SLANTED & ENCHANTED
Weekly Awards for the week ending August 30th, 1992

Good Team of the Week
The Butt Mountain Beavis were 7-0 for the week, scoring 38 runs and allowing
20.  The Barcelona Brewers had the strongest run margin at +25 runs, scoring
42 and allowing only 17 runs.

Bad Team of the Week
Both the Reykjavik 101ers and Grain Belt Premiums were each 1-6 during the week.  
Reykjavik, however, had the worse run margin at -19 runs.  

Hitter of the Week
Butt Mountain's Andre Dawson created 12.7 runs during the week.  In 29 plate
appearances he hit 464/483/929.  Runners up were Ferlin's Marquis Grissom, 
who created 12.4 runs and Barcelona's Daryl Boston with 11.3 runs created.

Bad Hitter of the Week
Baraboo's Jody Reed put up a .122 OPS in 25 plate appearances.	

Starter of the Week
Ferdinand's Hipolito Pichardo made THREE starts, totaling 15.2 innings.  He
allowed just 6 hits and 2 walks while striking out 7.

Reliever of the Week
Wilken's Bob Scanlan tossed 4.2 innings, giving up just a walk.

Bad pitcher of the Week 
Reykjavik's Bill Sampen gave up 12 runs on 12 hits and 3 walks in just 5.0
innings.
