SLANTED & ENCHANTED
Weekly Awards for the Week ending April 26th.

Good Team of the Week
The Butt Mountain Beavis were a perfect 7-0 for the week, scoring 42
runs and allowing 22.  Evanston (4-1) allowed just 9 runs in five games.

Bad Team of the Week
The Reykjavik 101ers were also perfect at 0-6 and scored 13 runs,
while allowing 32.  Their -19 run margin was not the league's worst,
however, as Nederland managed to be outscored by 21 runs in 6 games.

Hitter of the Week
Effin's Shane Mack created 10.9 runs while putting up a 1.230 OPS with
a line of 536/552/679.  Dave Justice was runner up, creating 10.2 runs.

Bad Hitter of the Week
Cincinnati's Lance Parrish put up a 0.000 OPS in 4 starts and 15 plate
appearances.

Starter of the Week
Butt Mountain's Greg Maddux allowed just 9 hits and 3 walks over 15
innings, while striking out 11.  And while he got no decisions for
either start, he gets the nod here.

Reliever of the Week
Grain Belt's Paul Quantrill tossed 9.2 innings over 5 appearances, 
allowing just 5 baserunners.

Bad pitcher of the Week 
Grain Belt's Donovan Osborn made two starts and lasted 4.2 innings
combined, while surrending 8 runs on 16 hits and 2 walks.  Somehow
he didn't allow a homerun.
