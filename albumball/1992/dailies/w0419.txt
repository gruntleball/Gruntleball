SLANTED & ENCHANTED
Weekly Awards for the Week ending April 19th.

Good Team of the Week
The Tewksbury Bobs went 5-1, and outscored the opposition by 25 runs,
43 to 18.  Cincinnati scored the most runs at 46 in six games, and 
allowed the fewest--12, but were 4-2.

Bad Team of the Week
The Nederland Frozen Dead Guys and Century Atoms were both 1-5 for the 
week, but Century had a worse run margin at -10.  Reykjavik allowed
the most runs and had the poorest run margin in the league at -40.
Effin scored the fewest runs--just 13 in five games.

Hitter of the Week
Cincinnati's Rob Deer created 13.5 runs--half again more than the 
runners-up Omar Vizquel and Jeff Bagwell at 8.5 runs.  Deer put up
a 1.730 OPS in 24 plate appearances.

Bad Hitter of the Week
Ken Caminiti, also of Cincinnati compiled a .204 OPS in 27 plate
appearances.	

Starter of the Week
Barcelona's Jaime Navarro tossed 13 innings and gave up just 2 runs on
7 hits and 4 walks, while striking out 8.  Dave Fleming of Dun Laoghaire
managed to get a loss despite allowing only 3 runs in 16.2 innings.

Reliever of the Week
Cincinnati's Todd Stottlemyre was lights out in his 4 appearances and
7.1 innings, allowing one hit, one walk and striking out 3.

Bad pitcher of the Week 
Sometimes a pitcher is just so bad, despite few innings, that it merits
mention here.  Reykjavik's Chuck McElroy allowed 9 runs (albeit 4 earned)
in just 1.2 innings, serving up a couple of long balls in the process.
