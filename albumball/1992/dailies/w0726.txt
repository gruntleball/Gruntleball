SLANTED & ENCHANTED
Weekly Awards for the week ending July 26th, 1992

Good Team of the Week
The Butt Mountain Beavis went 6-1 for the week, scoring a league-high 49 runs 
and allowing 26.  The Dun Laoghaire Messiahs led the league in run prevention,
allowing just 10 runs in six games.

Bad Team of the Week
The Reykjavik 101ers were 0-7 for the week, no doubt aided by playing with a 
short roster (yes, that's a hint).  Reykjavik averaged just over 2 runs a game
and allowed an average over 5 runs.

Hitter of the Week
Butt Mountain's Andre Dawson created 11.7 runs with a batting line of 419/455/871
in 33 plate appearances.  Last week's winner Wally Joyner had another strong week,
creating 10.4 runs with a 1.202 OPS.  Darren Daulton was 3rd runner up with 10.3
runs created.

Bad Hitter of the Week
Grain Belt's Gary DiSarcina managed a .154 OPS in 26 plate appearances.

Starter of the Week
Butt Mountain's Roger Clemens finally gets his weekly award, throwing 16.0 innings.
He allowed 2 runs on 10 hits and 6 walks and struck out 17.  Honorable mentions to 
Dennis Martinez, Scott Sanderson and John Smoltz.

Reliever of the Week
Houston's Mel Rojas tossed 6.2 shutout innings of relief, giving up one hit and one
walk, striking out three.

Bad pitcher of the Week 
Nederland's Frank Tanana gave up 11 runs in 10.0 innings in which he allowed 15 hits
and five walks.
