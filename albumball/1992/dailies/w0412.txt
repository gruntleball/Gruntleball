SLANTED & ENCHANTED
Weekly Awards for the Week ending April 12th.

Good Team of the Week
The Butt Mountain Beavis were a perfect 6-0 for the week, allowing
only nine runs while scoring 24.  Dun Laoghaire had the largest run
margin at 45 to 16, but somehow managed to lose a game.

Bad Team of the Week
The Century Atoms were a perfect 0-7 and were outscored by 38 runs,
54 to 16.

Hitter of the Week
Needmore's Danny Tartabull was just nuts, putting up a 1.844 OPS and
17.9 RC for the week.  Andy Van Slyke and Barry Bonds created 13.0 and
11.6 runs, respectively.

Bad Hitter of the Week
Effin's Pat Kelly had a .118 OPS for the week in 17 plate appearances.

Starter of the Week
Barcelona's Curt Schilling pitched 16 innings and gave up just 8 hits,
2 walks and struck out 12.

Reliever of the Week
Barcelona's Jeff Montgomery appeared in four games, tossing six innings,
allowing just four hits and striking out six.

Bad pitcher of the Week 
Needmore's Chuck Finley allowed 24 baserunners in 7.2 innings, which 
resulted in 13 runs against him.
