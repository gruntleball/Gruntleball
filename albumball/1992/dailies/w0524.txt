SLANTED & ENCHANTED
Weekly Awards for the Week ending May 24th.

Good Team of the Week
The Houston Streets were 5-1 for the week with a run margin of +22 
runs.  No other team so dominated its opponents.  Three teams were 5-2.  

Bad Team of the Week
The Century Atoms went 1-6 and were outscored by 22 runs.  The Nederland
Frozen Dead Guys were also 1-6 but were outscored by only 15 runs.

Hitter of the Week
Ferdinand's Barry Bonds created a more reasonable 11.4 runs in 30 plate 
appearances to win for the second straight week.  His OPS was 1.393.
Runners up included Tim Raines, Shane Mack, Phil Clark and Carney Lansford,
all with over 10.0 RC.

Bad Hitter of the Week
Nederland's Ivan Rodriguez has come back to earth, managing just a .227 OPS
over the past week in 24 plate appearances.

Starter of the Week
Baraboo's Charles Nagy pitched two games and 14.1 innings, allowing only 9
hits and 1 walk, while striking out 11.

Reliever of the Week
Ferdinand's Rich Monteleone pitched 6.0 innings in 5 games, allowing just 1
hit and no walks while striking out six.

Bad pitcher of the Week 
Cincinnati's Andy Benes lasted 5.1 innings over two starts, allowing 12 hits
and six walks, striking out no one and giving up a homerun--all of which led
to 12 runs allowed.  Ooof.
