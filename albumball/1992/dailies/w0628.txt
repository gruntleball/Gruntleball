SLANTED & ENCHANTED
Weekly Awards for the week ending June 28th

Good Team of the Week
The Reykjavik 101ers (!) were 6-1 for the week, and outscored their opponents
30-17.  The Wilken Tools Police were also 6-1, but their run margin was inferior
to the mighty 101ers.  Dun Laoghaire had the greatest margin at +18 runs.

Bad Team of the Week
Lots of 2-5 teams this week.  The Ferlin Huskies get the nod for being outscored
30-17.

Hitter of the Week
Barcelona's Robin Ventura and Brady Anderson had outstanding weeks.  Ventura
created 12.3 runs and Brady 12.0 runs.

Bad Hitter of the Week
Houston's Manuel Lee managed a .232 OPS in 23 plate appearances.

Starter of the Week
Tewksbury's Mark Guthrie threw 14.1 innings, including 6.1 perfect innings against
Chinatrust.  He allowed just 4 hits and 5 walks, striking out 14.

Reliever of the Week
Ferlin's Rusty Meacham appeared in 3 games, threw 5.2 innings, and allowed just 2 
hits and a walk, striking out 4.

Bad pitcher of the Week 
Needmore's Chuck Finley made two starts and one relief appearance.  He pitched
6.2 innings, allowed 10 hits, 11 walks and 10 runs.
