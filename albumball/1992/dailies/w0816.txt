SLANTED & ENCHANTED
Weekly Awards for the week ending August 16th, 1992

Good Team of the Week
The Butt Mountain Beavis and the Ferlin Huskies were each 6-0 for the week.
Butt Mountain was just a bit more dominant, however, with a 32 run margin
compared to 26 for Ferlin.

Bad Team of the Week
The Reykjavik 101ers were 1-6 and were outscored by 33 runs.  The Atoms and
the Whales were each 1-4 for the week.

Hitter of the Week
Butt Mountain's Rene Gonzales created 12.1 runs with a 1.600 OPS in 21 plate
appearances.  Runners up were Edgar Martinez (11.6), Brady Anderson (11.6) and
Jeff Blauser (10.2).

Bad Hitter of the Week
Evanston's Charlie Hayes managed a .203 OPS in 25 plate appearances.

Starter of the Week
Ferdinand's Zane Smith made two starts totaling 17 innings pitched.  He allowed
3 runs on 11 hits and one walk, and struck out eight.  Runners up were Dun
Laoghaire's Tom Glavine who pitched a complete game shutout, and Baraboo's Ricky
Bones, who allowed just three hits and a walk and struck out eight in his
complete game.

Reliever of the Week
Cincinnati's Todd Worrell pitched 5.1 innings, allowing no hits and two walks
while striking out four.

Bad pitcher of the Week 
Evanston's Jim Deshaies gave up 10 runs in only 3.2 innings.
