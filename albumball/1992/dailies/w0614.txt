SLANTED & ENCHANTED
Weekly Awards for the Week ending June 14th.

Good Team of the Week
The Barcelona Brewers went 6-1 for the week, scoring 35 runs and allowing
just 17.

Bad Team of the Week
Both the Reykjavik 101ers and Nederland Frozen Dead Guys were 1-6, but 
RYK was outscored 42-22, compared to 34-27 for NFG.

Hitter of the Week
Chinatrust's Larry Walker created 13.9 runs for the week, putting up a 
1.444 OPS in 27 plate appearances.  Marquis Grissom (11.9) and Tim Raines
(11.0) were runners up.

Bad Hitter of the Week
Ferdinand's Chito Martinez created 0.2 runs with a .176 OPS in 17 plate
appearances.

Starter of the Week
Baraboo's Ricky Bones pitched 14.0 innings and gave up one run.  He 
allowed just 6 hits and 3 walks while striking out 13.

Reliever of the Week
Baraboo's Mike Jackson threw 5.1 innings, allowed 2 hits, no walks and
struck out 4.  Ferdinand's Mike Perez was also solid, giving up 3 hits
and no walks and striking out 4.  Close call in favor Jackson.

Bad pitcher of the Week 
Tewksbury's Steve Avery gave up 13 runs on 14 hits and 6 walks in 8.1 
innings over two games.
