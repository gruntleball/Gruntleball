SLANTED & ENCHANTED
Weekly Awards for the Week ending May 3rd.

Good Team of the Week
The Ferdinand Magellans went 5-1 with a run margin of +10, scoring 
36 runs and allowing 26.  For the week, the Chinatrust Whales had 
the best run margin at +15 runs and were 5-2.

Bad Team of the Week
The Century Atoms were 0-7 and were outscored by 16 runs.  The Baraboo
Goitermen had the worst margin at -20 runs, scoring 13 and allowing 33.

Hitter of the Week
Chinatrust's Robby Thompson created 13.5 runs in 30 plate appearances
with an OPS of 1.429 for the week.  He hit 3 HR, scored 8 and drove in 7.

Bad Hitter of the Week
Baraboo's Kenny Lofton created zero runs and had a .105 OPS in 29 plate
appearances, striking out 7 times.

Starter of the Week
Tewksbury's Chris Bosio allowed no runs in 16 innings and just 10 base-
runners reached.

Reliever of the Week
For the 2nd straight week, Grain Belt's Paul Quantrill gets the nod for
not only pitching effectively, but pitching lots.  He tossed 10.2 innings
over 5 appearances, allowing just 7 baserunners and striking out 8.

Bad pitcher of the Week 
Needmore's Erik Hanson had a rough week, lasting just 4.1 innings but
giving up 11 runs on 13 hits, 4 walks and 3 homeruns allowed.
